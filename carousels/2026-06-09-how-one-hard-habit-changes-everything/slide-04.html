Tip 3

Let it be
ugly at first

You will do it badly. The set will be light. The page will be thin. The prayer will feel like talking to a wall.

Do it anyway.

  • Show up before you've earned the right to look good.
  • Consistency compounds. Perfection never starts.

The man who shows up badly wins over the man who waited to show up well.
