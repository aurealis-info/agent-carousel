Tip 2

Anchor it to
something already sacred

A new habit dies without a host.

Tie it to something you already do: the morning, the coffee, the commute, the prayer. Stack it on a routine that already holds.

  • When the anchor is faith, the habit stays tethered to something that matters.
  • One page of Scripture before the feed is not a morning routine — it's a war move.

The habit that follows your prayer lives longer than the one that follows your alarm.
