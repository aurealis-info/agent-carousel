Step 2

Small and unseen is the test, not the exception

"Whoever can be trusted with very little can also be trusted with much." Luke 16:10. Not whoever nails the big opportunity when it finally shows up.

God isn't waiting for a bigger stage to build a man. He's doing it in the driveway with the oil pan, in the drawer that still needs fixing, in the email he keeps avoiding.

  • The task feels small because it is small. That's the point.
  • Faithful in little is the qualifying round for faithful in much.

He's not withholding the big thing from you. He's forming you for it.
