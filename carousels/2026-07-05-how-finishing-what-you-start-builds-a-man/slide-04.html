Step 3

Finish the thing in front of you before you start the next one

Pick one abandoned task today. One, not five. The half-painted fence. The resume. The conversation you keep postponing until it's convenient.

Finish it before you let yourself start anything new. That rule alone will expose how many things you start just to feel productive, then quit the second it gets boring.

  • One unfinished thing, closed out today. No new project until it is.

Discipline isn't starting strong. It's closing the loop.
