Step 4

This is the man it's quietly building

A man who finishes the boring things becomes a man his word means something to. His wife believes what he says he'll do. His friends stop bracing for the follow-through that never comes.

That's not a productivity hack. That's the shape a man takes when God has been forming him one closed loop at a time, not because he had to prove himself, but because that's what a finished, faithful life looks like from the inside.

  • Trustworthy isn't a personality. It's a track record.
  • Every finished small thing is a brick in a man people can build on.

Finish the small thing. Let God build the man.
