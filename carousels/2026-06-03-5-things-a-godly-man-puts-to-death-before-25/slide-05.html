Thing 4

Envy in disguise

He's scrolling another man's life and calling it motivation. It isn't.

Envy makes you work hard for the wrong reason — and resent the man who has what you want. Run your lane. God didn't build you to live someone else's life.

  • Comparison is a substitute for purpose.
  • The man chasing his calling doesn't have bandwidth to resent the man next to him.

Kill comparison, or it kills your direction.
