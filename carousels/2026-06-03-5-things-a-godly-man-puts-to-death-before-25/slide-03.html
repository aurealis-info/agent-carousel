Thing 2

Waiting for permission

He doesn't start the hard conversation. Doesn't make the call. Stays in the job, the habit, the fog — waiting for something to make the choice obvious.

Nobody's coming. And passivity isn't peace; it's cowardice wearing patience's clothes.

  • The called man goes first — into the cost, into the unknown.
  • Decide. Even badly. Movement beats drift every time.

A man who waits for permission will always be led by whoever gives it.
