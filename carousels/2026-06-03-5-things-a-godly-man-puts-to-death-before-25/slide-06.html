Thing 5

Soft prayer

Telling God how hard it is. Never getting up.

That's not humility — it's delay with a spiritual wrapper. Lament is real. Wallowing is a choice.

"In his anguish he prayed more earnestly." — Luke 22:44. Jesus sweat blood and still chose obedience.

  • Self-pity dressed as prayer is still self-pity.
  • Humility shows up. Humility moves.

Get up. The day has work in it.
