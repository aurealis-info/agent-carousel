  1. 6. He talks less about what he's doing and just does it.

    Announcement and action don't usually travel together.

  2. 7. He carries other men's burdens without keeping score.

    "Bear one another's burdens." — Galatians 6:2. Not "until it costs you."

  3. 8. He's hard on his own sin and generous with others'.

    He applies grace outward the way he wishes someone would apply it to him.

  4. 9. He doesn't need to be the main character.

    He's comfortable making someone else's story better.

  5. 10. He knows God's working in him and doesn't have to perform otherwise.

    Honest about his weakness — because his strength doesn't come from the show.
