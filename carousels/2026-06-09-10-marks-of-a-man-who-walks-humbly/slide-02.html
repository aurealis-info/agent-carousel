  1. 1. He can look any man in the eye and still get on his knees.

    Confidence and humility are the same man — one faces God, one faces men.

  2. 2. He doesn't need the credit to do the work.

    He knows who sees.

  3. 3. He asks for help before he needs rescuing.

    Pride waits until it's drowning. Humility asks while it can still swim.

  4. 4. He takes correction without litigating it.

    A humble man can hear "you're wrong" without turning it into a trial.

  5. 5. He's the same size in every room.

    He doesn't shrink for small rooms or swell for large ones.
