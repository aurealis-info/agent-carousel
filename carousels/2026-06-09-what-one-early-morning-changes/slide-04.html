Habit 3

Move Your Body

Your body is the instrument God uses to do the work He built you for. Treat it that way.

Not performance. Not aesthetics. Function. Twenty minutes is enough. The point isn't the workout — it's showing up before you feel like it. That's the discipline. The body just happens to benefit too.

  • Do something hard before you do anything comfortable.
  • The man who moves in the morning moves differently all day.
