Step 4

Renew the decision every morning

A decision made once doesn't hold forever. The man who decided last Monday isn't automatically the same man today.

Spiritual formation is a daily practice. The governed man sets the tone before the day sets it for him.

  • Open your Bible before your phone. Feed the right thing first.
  • Pray before you plan. The morning is where governance is built or lost.

The man who wins the first hour usually wins the day.
