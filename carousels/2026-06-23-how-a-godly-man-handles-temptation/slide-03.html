Step 2

Build structure before the moment hits

Resolve collapses under pressure. Structure holds.

The man who redesigns his environment beats temptation before it shows up. Move the phone. Change the route. Build an evening that doesn't end in the same place.

  • Set the boundary before the pressure arrives, not during it.
  • One structural change beats a hundred white-knuckle victories.

The environment always wins eventually. Build the right one.
