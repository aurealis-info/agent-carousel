Step 3

Replace the hour, don't just empty it

What you remove leaves a gap. Something fills it.

The man who quits the habit without filling the time almost always comes back. The battle isn't just won by subtraction.

  • Know what goes in the slot before you clear the slot.
  • Prayer, a walk, a call to a brother. Anything that costs something.

You're not just breaking a habit. You're building a man.
