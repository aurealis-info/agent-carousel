Step 1

Name what's actually pulling you

Willpower says: resist harder. Governance asks: what is this pointing at?

Temptation finds the unmet need, the unhealed wound, the hour with no plan. It isn't random.

Before you try to fight the surface, name the real thing underneath. The boredom. The loneliness. The pride looking for a place to land.

You can't govern what you haven't named.
