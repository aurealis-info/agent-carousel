Step 1

Call it what it is

You've been calling it a struggle, a habit, a thing you're working on. That language buys it time.

Scripture doesn't say manage your sin. It says put it to death. Those are two different projects.

  • A struggle gets sympathy. A death sentence gets action.
  • Say the real word for it tonight, even just to yourself.

You can't kill what you're still calling a struggle.
