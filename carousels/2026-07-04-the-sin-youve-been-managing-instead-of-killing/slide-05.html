Step 4

Fill the room you emptied

Killing it isn't the finish line. An empty room doesn't stay empty. Something moves back in, usually the same thing, unless you put something better there first.

Put the discipline in before the void does. The early prayer, the gym, the call to a brother instead of the scroll.

  • Decide tonight what fills the space this thing used to own.
  • Put it in the calendar, not just in your head.

An empty house doesn't stay empty. Fill it first.
