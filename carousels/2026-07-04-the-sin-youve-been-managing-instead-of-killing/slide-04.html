Step 3

Say it to a man, not just to God

You've confessed it in your head a hundred times. That's not the same as a brother knowing it and staying anyway.

"Confess your sins to one another, that you may be healed." What you keep in the dark keeps its grip. What you say out loud starts losing it.

  • Pick one man. Tell him the actual thing, not the softened version.
  • Ask him to check on it, not just hear about it once.

Nothing you're hiding is dying. It's just waiting.
