Step 2

Cut the supply, not just the symptom

You keep fighting the moment it shows up instead of the thing that keeps feeding it. That's why it keeps winning at the same hour, on the same app, in the same room.

Find what supplies it. The late scroll, the empty schedule, the account you still follow. Cut the source before you white-knuckle the symptom again.

  • Name the one thing that feeds it every single time.
  • Remove access tonight, not "when you feel stronger."

You don't out-discipline a supply line. You cut it.
