Step 3

Don't apologize for the room you're in

"Whoever exalts himself will be humbled, and whoever humbles himself will be exalted." (Matthew 23:12)

That's a promise about position, not about hiding.

  • Take the seat you were given.
  • Don't perform smallness to seem holy.

Shrinking isn't the same thing as being low before God.
