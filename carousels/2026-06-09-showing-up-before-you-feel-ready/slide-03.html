The body doesn't build on the days you feel like it

Any man can train when he's fired up. The man who becomes dangerous is the one who shows up when it's early, it's cold, and nobody's watching.

Discipline is built in the gap between not wanting to and going anyway.

  • Move before you negotiate with yourself.
  • The rep you do when you don't want to is worth three.
