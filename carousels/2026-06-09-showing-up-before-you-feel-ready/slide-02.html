Waiting is the slowest way to grow

Most men are waiting for the right moment. Waiting to feel motivated before they train. Waiting to feel called before they lead. Waiting to feel prepared before they commit.

But readiness isn't a feeling — it's a decision.

  • You'll never feel ready enough.
  • The man who moves anyway builds what the waiter never will.

Motivation follows action. Not the other way around.
