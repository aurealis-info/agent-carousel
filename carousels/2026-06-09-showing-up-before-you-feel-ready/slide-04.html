Step 3

Do it badly before you do it well

God doesn't wait for you to have the sermon polished before He sends you to preach it. He sends you while you're still learning to use your voice.

The first try is supposed to be messy. That's not failure — that's how it works.

  • Imperfect action beats perfect inaction.
  • He can't steer a parked car.

Obedience doesn't wait for confidence. It builds it.
