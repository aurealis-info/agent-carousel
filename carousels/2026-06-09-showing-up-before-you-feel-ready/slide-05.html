Step 4

What showing up actually builds

Showing up before you feel ready doesn't just get work done. It builds the kind of man who trusts his own word. Who doesn't need conditions to move. Who knows that when God calls him, he'll go.

  • Every time you go anyway, you build a little more trust in yourself.
  • That trust is what God uses to build something through you.

The man who showed up built the man who was ready.
