Step 1

Name what's actually feeding it

Most men fight the scroll with willpower. Willpower is thin by 9 PM.

The real fuel isn't desire. It's empty time, a mind with nowhere to go and something waiting to fill it.

  • An idle hour is a door left open.
  • Boredom is where the fight actually gets lost.

You don't win the moment. You win the hour before it.
