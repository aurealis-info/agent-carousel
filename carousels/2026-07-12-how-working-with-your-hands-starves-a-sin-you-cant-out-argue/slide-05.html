Step 4

Watch what stops needing killing

You stop white-knuckling the 11 PM scroll because 11 PM finds you tired from something real.

This is what Colossians means by putting sin to death. Not managed. Not negotiated with. Starved until it stops showing up.

  • A sin with nothing to feed on stops fighting back.
  • The work isn't the reward. It's the war.

Starve it before you try to outrun it.
