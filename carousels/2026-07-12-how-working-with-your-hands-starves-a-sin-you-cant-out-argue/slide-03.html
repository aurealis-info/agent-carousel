Step 2

Give your hands a job before your mind finds one

Scripture never called work the curse. It called it the ground he was made to work, before anything went wrong.

A task with your hands crowds out the appetite that lives in your head.

  • Fix something. Build something. Move something heavy.
  • A body that's occupied is a mind that's harder to steal.

Busy hands, quiet war.
