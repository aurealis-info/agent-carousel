Step 1

Fix your eyes on God, not on her

Desperation leaks. Women can sense the man who needs to be chosen.

The man who chases God hard enough forgets to perform for an audience.

  • Stop checking if she noticed. Start checking if God's pleased.
  • Your hunger for approval is what she'll run from — and rightly so.

The man who needs her to feel complete is a burden, not a leader.
