Step 3

Become the kind of man who can lead

Leadership costs you first. First to apologize. First to serve. First to take the hit.

If you can't lead yourself — your schedule, your body, your tongue — you can't lead a home.

  • Lead your own life before you ask to lead hers.
  • The man who leads well learned how to follow Christ first.

Real leadership isn't a title. It's the willingness to go first into the cost.
