Step 3

Jesus was meek, not mild

He wept with the grieving. He ate with the men everyone wrote off.

He also braided a whip and cleared the temple. Told the religious leaders exactly what they were, to their faces.

Meek means your strength is under authority. Mild means you have no strength. Don't confuse them — Jesus didn't.

Gentleness in the right place. Unmovable in the right place. That's the man.
