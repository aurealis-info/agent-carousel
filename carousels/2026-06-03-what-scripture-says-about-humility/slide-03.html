Step 2

The humble man isn't passive

Read the Psalms. David was on his face before God and unmoved before Goliath — same man, same week.

That's the picture Scripture paints. Not a man who backs down. A man who knows who holds him.

  • Getting on your knees before God isn't softness.
  • It's where you find out what you're actually made of.

The man who knows who holds him doesn't need to prove himself to anyone.
