Step 4

It's how you see clearly

"Pride goes before destruction, and a haughty spirit before a fall." — Proverbs 16:18.

The proud man can't receive correction. Can't be coached. Can't see himself as he actually is. He's locked inside his own story, and nothing true gets in.

  • Humility opens the door to growth.
  • Pride locks it from the inside.

The man on his knees sees further than the man on his throne.
