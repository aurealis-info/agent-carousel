Step 1

You don't think less of yourself

Humility isn't a campaign against yourself.

C.S. Lewis put it plainly: the humble man isn't the one who thinks poorly of himself — he's the one who stopped thinking about himself so much.

  • Insecurity performs itself. Humility just gets on with it.
  • The humble man isn't small. He's just not the center.

Self-forgetfulness is not the same as self-contempt.
