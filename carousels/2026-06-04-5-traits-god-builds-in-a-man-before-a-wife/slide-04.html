Trait 3

Go first into the cost

Leadership isn't having the opinion. It's going first into the hard thing.

The apology. The awkward conversation. The decision nobody wants to own.

  • She's not looking for a man who tells her what to do.
  • She's looking for a man who goes first — before it's easy, before he feels ready.

Real leadership cost Christ his life. Yours will cost you something too.
