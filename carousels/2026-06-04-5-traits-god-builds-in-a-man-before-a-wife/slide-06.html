Get men who know your sin

Accountability isn't a podcast. It's two or three actual men with your number who use it.

A man who keeps his sin private takes it into the marriage. It doesn't disappear. It waits.

  • "Iron sharpens iron." — Proverbs 27:17. You need the friction before the ring.
  • Find the brothers who are allowed to see who you actually are.

You can't be the man she needs if nobody's allowed to see you.
