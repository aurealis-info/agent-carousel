Trait 2

Pray without an audience

Not because she's watching. Not because a group is going.

A man whose faith only runs hot in public is building on sand — and she'll figure it out before you do.

  • Get in the Word because God is real to you — not to look like the guy who does.
  • Private devotion builds the man the public never sees. That man is the real one.

She needs a man whose faith holds when the lights go down.
