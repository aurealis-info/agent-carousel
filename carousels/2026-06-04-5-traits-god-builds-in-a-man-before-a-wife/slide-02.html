Govern yourself first

A man who can't say no to himself can't be trusted with a household.

The discipline — get up, put the phone down, kill the private tab — that's not impressive. It's table stakes.

  • The hidden stuff doesn't stay hidden. It comes out in the marriage.
  • Self-mastery isn't optional. It's the foundation everything else sits on.

A man who runs himself can be trusted with everything else.
