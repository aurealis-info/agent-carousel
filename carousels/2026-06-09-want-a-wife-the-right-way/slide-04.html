Step 3

The order is the whole thing

Chase God first. Become the man He's forming — disciplined, rooted, honest with at least one other man about your sin.

Then the woman worth having shows up in the field you're already in.

The invitation isn't "when you're perfect, love will come." It's "when your life is ordered, you'll recognize what you're looking for."

  • "Seek first the kingdom of God, and all these things will be added." — Matthew 6:33

The pursuit of God is the best preparation for marriage.
