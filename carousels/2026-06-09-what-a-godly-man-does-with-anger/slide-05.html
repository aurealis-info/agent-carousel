Step 4

Let it land where it belongs

"Be angry and do not sin." — Ephesians 4:26. He didn't say stop being angry. He said don't sin in it.

There's a clean anger that protects what matters and corrects what's wrong. And there's a dirty anger that just uses the people you love as targets.

  • Know which one you're carrying before you carry it into the room.

Anger in a governed man is useful. In an ungoverned one, it's a weapon he turns on everyone who loves him.
