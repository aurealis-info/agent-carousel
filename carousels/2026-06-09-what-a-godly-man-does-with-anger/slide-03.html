Step 2

Direct it, don't bury it

A man who buries his anger doesn't deal with it — he defers the damage. It comes out sideways: at the wrong person, the wrong moment, with more force than it should have carried.

Buried anger doesn't stay buried. It runs you from underground.

  • Don't manage it alone and call it strength.
  • Bring it to God before you bring it to the room.
