Thing 2

A body you govern

Your body belongs to God — stewardship is worship, not vanity.

The man who can’t govern himself loses ground in every other fight.

  • Train it for endurance, for service, for the long fight.
  • The man who governs his body can be trusted with other things.

You can’t lead from a body you’ve surrendered to comfort.
