A prayer life that’s real

Not the version you show — the version you live at 5 AM with no audience.

That’s where the man of God is built. Ordinary. Unglamorous. Day after day.

  • Seek him before you open anything else.
  • The man who meets God in the quiet carries him into the loud.

Do not grow weary in doing good.
— Galatians 6:9
