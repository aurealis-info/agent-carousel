  1. 5. He admits when he doesn't know.

    The man secure enough to say "I don't know yet" is easier to follow than the one who bluffs.

  2. 6. He prays before he decides.

    Not as a ritual. As a real pause that puts God in the room before the choice gets made.

  3. 7. He gives credit he'll never collect.

    A man of God points to God and to the people around him — not to himself. That's the last thing most men learn.
