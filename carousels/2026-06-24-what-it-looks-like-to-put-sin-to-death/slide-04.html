Step 3

Bring one man into it

James 5:16 isn't a suggestion. It's the mechanism.

What you confess in the light loses the power it has in the dark. Find one man. Tell him the actual truth — not the edited version, not the framing that makes you look like you're already winning.

The whole truth. The specific one.

Light is the only thing that breaks the grip.
