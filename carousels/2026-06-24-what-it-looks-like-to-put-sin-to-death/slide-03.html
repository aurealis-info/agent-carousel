Step 2

Kill it before it wakes up

Sin has a schedule. Learn it.

Most men fight the temptation when it's already in the room — when their resolve is lowest and the pull is strongest. The man who survives cuts off the path before he's on it.

  • Win the first hour of the morning. Every day.
  • Know your weak hours. Don't walk into them unprepared.

Don't fight sin at its strongest. Meet it before it gets there.
