Step 1

Name what you're actually killing

"Struggling with lust" and "killing lust" are different postures.

One keeps the war at a polite distance. The other gets specific — the exact habit, the exact hour, the exact device. You can't kill what you won't name.

  • Vague confession makes vague progress.
  • Name the sin before you claim to be fighting it.

The man who knows the name knows where to aim.
