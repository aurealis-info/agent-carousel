  1. 5. They pray for you by name, not in general.

    "Unspoken" is about shame. A man with real brothers can name his actual need.

  2. 6. They hold you to what you said you were going to do.

    Brotherhood without accountability is just friendship. Good, but not enough.

  3. 7. They'd get in a car if you called at midnight.

    Not "let me know if you need anything." They'd come. You both know it.

  4. 8. They talk about God together like it's natural.

    Not only at Bible study. At lunch, on the drive, in the group chat. Faith woven into the friendship itself.
