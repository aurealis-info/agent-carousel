Step 1

Your habits don't care how you feel

Motivation is weather. It comes and goes, and the man who builds on it builds nothing solid.

Scripture never says wait until you feel ready. It says walk faithfully with what's already in your hand.

  • The discipline you built last month carries you this morning.
  • Build the system. Then trust it to carry you when the feeling doesn't.

Motivation is weather. Discipline is infrastructure.
