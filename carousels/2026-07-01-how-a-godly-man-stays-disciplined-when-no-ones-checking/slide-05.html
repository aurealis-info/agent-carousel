Step 4

Let God govern what you can't

Self-control isn't self-generated. Galatians calls it fruit of the Spirit for a reason. You don't grit your way to it. You abide your way to it.

Every night you fail, that's not proof you're disqualified. It's proof you're still leaning on yourself instead of Him.

  • Before the moment of temptation, ask Him for it directly. Not after.

The man who runs his own life first learned to hand it over.
