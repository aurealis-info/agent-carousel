Step 3

Build walls before you need them

A city with no walls falls to whatever shows up. Willpower alone runs out around 11 PM.

Delete the app before the urge hits, not during it. Charge your phone across the room. Decide the boundary sober, not tempted.

  • Name your 11 PM version of yourself and build for him specifically.

Discipline isn't fighting the moment. It's rigging the moment in advance.
