Step 1

Name the urge. Don't obey it.

An urge shows up uninvited. What you do next is the only part that's actually you.

The scroll, the second plate, the snooze button, the thing you know you shouldn't open. None of it makes you weak. Feeding it does.

  • Say the urge out loud in your head: "I want to check my phone."
  • Then wait ten seconds before you decide.

You're not your urges. You're what you do after them.
