  1. 1. Pray. Out loud if you have to.

    Give God the first thought of your day. The feed gets plenty — He gets the first one.

  2. 2. Make your bed.

    Win the first rep before the day has a chance to say hello.

  3. 3. Read one page of Scripture.

    Not a study. A first word in a conversation you want running all day long.

  4. 4. Move your body — even 15 minutes.

    You owe your mind a body that's awake before you ask it to carry the day.

  5. 5. Write down one thing you're grateful for.

    Gratitude and anxiety can't both run the first five minutes. Pick one.
