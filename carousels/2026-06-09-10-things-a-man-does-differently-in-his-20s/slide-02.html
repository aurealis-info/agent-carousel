  1. 1. He reads something that challenges him.

    Not content that confirms what he already thinks. Books that argue back.

  2. 2. He lifts when he doesn't want to.

    The rep you force on the bad day builds more than the one on the good day.

  3. 3. He earns his own opinion.

    Read the original, not the summary. Form a view before you get one handed to you.

  4. 4. He confesses sin to one man.

    Not a prayer request. One man, face to face, who's allowed to ask the follow-up question.

  5. 5. He learns to be uncomfortable and stay.

    Hard conversations. Silence. Cold water. Proof he can be trusted with difficult things.
