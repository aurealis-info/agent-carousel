The wrong order

Build your finances. Hit the gym. Get a plan.

None of that is wrong. But there’s a question underneath all of it: build for who?

A man who builds for God has a reason. A man who builds for her has a performance.

  • Performances end. Foundations don’t.

The foundation you build for God is the one she can actually stand on.
