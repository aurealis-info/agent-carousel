What Paul learned

“I have learned, in whatever state I am, to be content.” — Philippians 4:11

Not born content. Learned it.

The man who can be alone with God — and not be restless — brings love into a relationship. Not need.

  • Stop asking her to fill what only God can.

Contentment is what makes you capable of love, not just longing.
