Step 1

The chase that repels

The man who needs a wife has already told her who he is.

She hears it in the first conversation — the pressure, the over-eagerness, the look that wants something from her.

That’s not love. That’s a man who made her responsible for his completeness.

  • You can’t lead from that place.

She doesn’t want to rescue you. She wants to choose a man who’s already whole.
