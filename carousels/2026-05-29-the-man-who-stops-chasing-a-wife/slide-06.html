Step 5

Seek first

“Seek first the kingdom of God and his righteousness, and all these things will be added to you.” — Matthew 6:33

Not “find her, then build your faith.”

Seek first. Become who God has been forming. The rest is gift, not goal.

  • There is only one first move.

Get the order right and you stop waiting — you start becoming.
