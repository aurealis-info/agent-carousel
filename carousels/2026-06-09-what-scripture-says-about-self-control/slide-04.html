Step 3

Control the Input

You don't fight lust by resisting it at 11 PM. You fight it by what you feed your mind at 7 AM.

Whatever you give your attention to first sets the frame for everything that follows. Scripture or the feed — one of them will run your mind. Pick which one gets first access.

  • What's the first thing you give your eyes to in the morning?
  • Discipline your appetite before the hunger peaks.
  • Upstream obedience is easier than downstream resistance.

Feed the man you want to be. Starve the one you're leaving behind.
