Step 2

Walk, Don't White-Knuckle

"Walk by the Spirit, and you will not gratify the desires of the flesh." — Galatians 5:16.

Notice the order. Walk first. The gratifying-the-flesh part is the outcome of not walking — not a separate battle you wage on its own turf.

Most men are fighting the desire at its peak. The fight should happen six hours earlier.

  • What does your morning look like before the temptation arrives?
  • The battle starts at the alarm, not the screen.

Win the morning and you've already fought the hardest part of the day.
