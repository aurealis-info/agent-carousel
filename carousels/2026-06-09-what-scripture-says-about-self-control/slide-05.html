Step 4

Win the Small Ones First

A man who can't be consistent in the small — making his bed, keeping his word to himself, putting the phone down — doesn't suddenly have discipline when it costs him something real.

The big victories are built on a thousand small ones you kept when no one was watching.

"Whoever is faithful in very little is also faithful in much." — Luke 16:10.

  • Pick one small thing you've been avoiding. Do it every day this week.
  • The man you trust tomorrow is the man you're building today.

Faithful in little. Trusted with much.
