Step 1

It's a Fruit, Not a Feat

Willpower is a finite resource. You know this because you run out of it by 9 PM.

Galatians 5 puts self-control in the fruit of the Spirit — not the disciplines of the flesh. You don't manufacture it. You cultivate the root it grows from.

The man trying to force self-control without the Spirit is trying to grow oranges in the dark.

  • You don't produce it. You cultivate the root and it appears.
  • Stop grinding. Start growing.

A fruit is grown, not gritted.
