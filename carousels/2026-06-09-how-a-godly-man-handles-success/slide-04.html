Step 3

Keep the men who tell you the truth

Don’t trade your accountability for an audience. Success makes it tempting.

The men who knew you when you had nothing — they’re still your best check.

  • Don’t outgrow your brotherhood. Outgrow your sin instead.
  • A man surrounded by yes is a man flying blind.

Surround yourself with men who love you enough to be honest. That’s not an upgrade — that’s armor.
