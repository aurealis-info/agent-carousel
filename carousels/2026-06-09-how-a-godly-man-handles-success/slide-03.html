Step 2

Don’t let a good season make you prayerless

The more your life works, the easier it is to stop depending on God.

That’s the trap. The man who needed grace to get here still needs it to stay.

  • A full calendar and an empty prayer life is a warning, not a win.
  • Busyness after a breakthrough is where men quietly drift.

Stay on your knees when you’re winning. That’s when it matters most.
