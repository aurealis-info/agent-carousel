Step 4

Give God the credit out loud

A man of faith who succeeds quietly and credits only himself isn’t humble. He’s afraid.

Say it plainly: God opened that door. You just showed up.

  • Testimony isn’t performance. It’s giving credit where it’s owed.
  • The man who says “God did this” out loud invites others into a story bigger than his.

A man who handles success the same way he handles failure — that’s the man worth following.
