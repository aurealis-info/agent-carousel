Step 1

Success reveals what you’ve been building in private

Promotion doesn’t make a man proud. It shows you the pride that was already there.

Who you are in a winning season is who you’ve been forming in the dark.

  • Before you celebrate, ask: did my character keep pace?
  • Don’t mistake a good circumstance for a good heart.

A man whose character outpaces his circumstances is the man who can hold what God gives him.
