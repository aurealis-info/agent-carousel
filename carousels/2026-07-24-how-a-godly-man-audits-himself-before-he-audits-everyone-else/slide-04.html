Step 3

Correct him like you'd want to be corrected

Galatians 6:1 says restore him gently, watching yourself so you're not tempted too.

Not gently as a mood you perform. Gently because you know exactly how it feels to need grace.

  • Check your own footing before you reach for his.
  • Say it like a brother, not a verdict.

The same rescue you'd want is the one you owe him.
