Trade the eye roll for the mirror

The friend who's always late. The coworker who always drops the ball. Easy to catalogue.

Harder to ask what you drop that nobody's tracking as closely as you track theirs.

  • You keep a list on everyone but yourself.
  • Start one on you instead.

You'll run out of patience for his flaws long before you finish counting your own.
