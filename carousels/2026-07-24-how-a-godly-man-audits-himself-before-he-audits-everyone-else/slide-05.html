Step 4

Confess yours before you name his

The brother who says "here's where I've failed at this too" before he says anything else earns the right to speak into it.

The one who skips that step just sounds superior.

  • Lead with your own failure, not his.
  • Let that be what makes him listen.

Hard on yourself, gentle with him. Most men have it backwards.
