The hidden version is the real one

You can perform the gym version, the group chat version, the small group version. You can't perform the alone version. That one just happens.

What you do when literally nothing is tracking you, no algorithm, no accountability partner, no verse-of-the-day reminder, that's the actual read on your character.

  • Don't ask "what would people think." Ask "what does this say I actually believe."

Character is what's left when the audience leaves.
