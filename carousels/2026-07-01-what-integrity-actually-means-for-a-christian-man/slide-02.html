Step 1

Integrity is addition, not acting

Integrity comes from the same root as "integer." A whole number. Undivided.

It's not a personality trait you either have or don't. It's whether the guy at church, the guy at work, and the guy alone at 1 AM add up to the same man.

  • Ask yourself: would today's version of me surprise last Sunday's version?

A divided man is at war with himself before anyone else gets to him.
