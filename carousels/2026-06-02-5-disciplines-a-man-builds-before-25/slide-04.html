Discipline 3

Show up before you feel ready

The job nobody's watching. The early hours nobody's clapping for.

Proverbs 10:4 is blunt: a slack hand makes a man poor. A diligent hand makes him rich — and that's not just money.

The 20s are where a man gets built. Show up now, before the stakes are high enough to punish you for not doing it.

  • Do the thing you've been putting off — today.
  • Nobody is coming to make you start.

Usefulness is built in private. Recognition is a bonus.
