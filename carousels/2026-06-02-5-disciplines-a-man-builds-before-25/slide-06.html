Discipline 5

Find your brothers

Not a group chat. Not a podcast community. Two or three real men who have your number, know your sin, and will show up.

Iron sharpens iron — Proverbs 27:17. You don't get sharp alone. You get comfortable. You get soft. You get good at hiding.

  • Text one man today: "I want to do life with you. Let's meet."
  • Let him ask the hard question. Then answer it honestly.

The man who has no brothers has no one to catch him when he drifts.
