Discipline 1

Own the morning

The phone doesn't own the first hour of your day — you do.

Before the world has its say, you've already had yours with God.

Bible open. Knees down. Five minutes before the noise starts.

  • Set the alarm 30 minutes earlier than you think you need.
  • No phone until after you've prayed.

The man who owns his morning owns his day.
