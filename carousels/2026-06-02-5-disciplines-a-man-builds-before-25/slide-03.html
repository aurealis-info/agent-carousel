Discipline 2

Kill what's killing you

Scripture doesn't say manage your sin. It says put it to death.

The porn. The scrolling at 1 AM. The lazy pattern you've named a "season."

You know what yours is. You've known for a while.

  • Name it out loud to one brother this week.
  • Confession isn't weakness — it's the thing that breaks the grip.

A man who can say no to himself can be trusted with everything else.
