Discipline 4

Stay in the Word every day

Not because you have to. Because the man you want to become thinks differently than you do now.

One chapter. Every morning. Do it for 90 days and watch how differently you read people, read the room, read yourself.

  • Romans. James. Proverbs. Start somewhere with teeth.
  • Read slow enough to let it land — this isn't a reading plan, it's a formation plan.

"Your word is a lamp to my feet." — Psalm 119:105. You don't navigate a dark decade without it.
