Name it exactly. Vague confessions don't stick.

"I struggle with lust" is not a confession. It's an admission designed to protect you from the specifics. What is it, exactly? When does it happen? What were you doing 30 minutes before? The more precisely you name it, the less power it holds. "Confess your sins to one another, that you may be healed." — James 5:16
