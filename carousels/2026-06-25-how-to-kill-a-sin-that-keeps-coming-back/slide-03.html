Remove the on-ramp. Don't fight at the door.

The phone in the bedroom isn't the sin — but it's the on-ramp. Most men try to fight the temptation in real time, after every condition is already set. That's a fight you're losing before it starts. Take the phone out of the room. Put the thing that enables it out of reach before the urge arrives. Remove the path, not just the destination.
