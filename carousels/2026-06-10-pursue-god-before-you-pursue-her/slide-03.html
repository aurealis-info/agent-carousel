Your twenties are a forge, not a waiting room

These years aren't a gap between school and the good stuff. This is where the man gets built — the discipline, the rootedness, the fear of God.

What you build now is what she walks into. If you spend it looking for her, you might find her. You'll just show up empty-handed.

  • What gets formed in you before her is the foundation after.

Build before you need it.
