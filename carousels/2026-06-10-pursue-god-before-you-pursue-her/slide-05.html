Step 4

Become the man first

There's a difference between finding a woman to marry and becoming a man worth marrying.

One is a transaction. The other is formation. One you can do by chasing. The other only happens in the work you do when you're not chasing anyone.

  • Work on your character, your faithfulness, your self-control.
  • Trust Him with the timing.

The right woman will recognize a man who's actually been somewhere.
