Step 1

Stop grasping

The man who needs a relationship reeks of it. She feels it before it says a word — not because she's cruel, but because she's looking for someone planted, not grasping.

A man who hasn't found his footing in God is looking for her to give him something she can't give.

  • Neediness is not a love language. It's a weight.
  • She can't complete what God hasn't started.

Your anchor goes first. Then you look for her.
