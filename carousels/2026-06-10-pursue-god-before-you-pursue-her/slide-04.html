Step 3

Seek first. The order is the structure.

"Seek first the kingdom of God, and all these things will be added to you." — Matthew 6:33

The order isn't a suggestion. It's a structure. Men who reverse it spend their lives chasing — and losing — both.

  • The man who has God first doesn't beg for what comes next.

Get the order right. The rest is a promise.
