Your word

If you say you’ll be there and you’re not, you’re building a reputation — just the wrong one.

Character isn’t built in the big moments. It’s built in the small ones you thought no one was clocking.

  • One commitment. Keep it.
  • Then another. Let that be the pattern.

Your word is your character, visible before you have enough else to show.
