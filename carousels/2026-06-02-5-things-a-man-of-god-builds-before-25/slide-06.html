Thing 5

Money that serves him

If where your paycheck goes determines your decisions, something other than Christ owns you.

Spend less than you make. Give first. Learn that order now, not when you have a family watching.

  • Budget it. Give 10%. Do it before you feel ready.
  • A faithful steward gets trusted with more — in every room.

Handle it faithfully now. The stakes only get higher.
