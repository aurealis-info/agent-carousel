  1. 1. You're harder on yourself than on anyone else.

    Not in a self-hating way — in a standards-climbing way. The gap between who you are and who you could be moves you.

  2. 2. You're getting slower to talk and faster to listen.

    The man who still talks to prove himself is still performing. You've stopped needing to win the room.

  3. 3. The people closest to you aren't afraid of you.

    Your anger hasn't become the weather of the house. Your family doesn't walk on eggshells.

  4. 4. You'd rather be corrected than comfortable.

    A man who can't take rebuke can't be improved. You've learned to receive it — even when it stings — as a gift.
