Step 3

Let it form you, not harden you

Disappointment does one of two things in a man: it makes him bitter, or it makes him humble. You don't get to choose whether it shapes you. You only get to choose how you respond while it does.

A bitter man blames. A humbled man asks what God is building.

  • Ask what He's constructing — not just what you're losing.
  • Suffering is not a detour. It might be the road.
