Step 1

Don't bury it

Disappointment isn't a lack of faith. Pretending you're fine when you're not is closer to one.

David didn't sanitize his prayers in the Psalms. He brought the whole thing — the grief, the confusion, the anger at the silence. God can handle what you're actually carrying.

  • Bring the real prayer, not the polished one.
  • Honesty is where healing starts — not performance.
