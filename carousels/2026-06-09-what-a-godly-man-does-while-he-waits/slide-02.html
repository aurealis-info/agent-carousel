Step 1

Stop performing your readiness

The guy rehearsing his pitch — leading with what he provides, what he's building, who he's becoming — is still auditioning.

A woman worth marrying doesn't need a résumé. She needs a man who's already someone.

  • Stop making yourself the project she's supposed to complete.
  • The man genuinely formed doesn't need to announce he's finished.

You don't perform your way into being the right man. You become him.
