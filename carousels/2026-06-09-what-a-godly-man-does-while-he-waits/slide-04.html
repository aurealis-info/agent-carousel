Step 3

Let brotherhood do the formation

A man trying to be formed by a woman he doesn't have yet is cutting in line. That work belongs to the brothers who know his actual sin and will stay in the room anyway.

Two or three men who have his number and won't let him drift — that's the formation season.

  • Iron sharpens iron. Get some iron in your life.
  • The man formed by brothers shows up better everywhere else.

Brotherhood shapes the man. The man then shapes everything else.
