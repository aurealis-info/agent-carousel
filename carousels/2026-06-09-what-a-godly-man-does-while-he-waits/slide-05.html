Step 4

Hold the desire open-handed

He wants a wife. He's allowed to want that. Scripture doesn't say kill the desire — it says don't put it on the throne.

The open hand holds the want without making it the center. Delight yourself in the Lord, and he will give you the desires of your heart. — Psalm 37:4.

  • Not "suppress and pretend." Trust the Giver with the gift's timing.
  • The man who's got his hands open can receive what God actually has for him.

He who finds a wife finds a good thing — but only if he was looking in the right direction first.
