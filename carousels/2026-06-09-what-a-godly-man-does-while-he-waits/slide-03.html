Step 2

Chase God, not a relationship

The man desperate for a wife reeks of it. She can tell in the first 20 minutes. The need behind every move gives him away.

The man walking with God is building something worth interrupting — not performing being worth it.

  • Chasing God is what forms the man she'd actually want.
  • You can't lead where you haven't first learned to follow.

The man chasing God becomes the man worth marrying. In that order.
