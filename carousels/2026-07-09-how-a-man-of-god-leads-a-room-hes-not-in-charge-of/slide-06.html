Step 5

Stay when it's easier to leave

Group chats go quiet. Friendships get awkward after a hard conversation. The easy move is to drift.

The man who stays in the room after it gets uncomfortable is the one people remember when it matters.

  • Show up to the next thing, especially the tense one.

You don't need a title to lead. You just have to be the one who stays.
