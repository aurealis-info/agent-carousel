Step 2

Own your twenty percent first

Most men wait to see who's more at fault before they apologize. That's not humility, that's accounting.

Owning your part first, even the small part, is what actually moves a conflict. It's not weakness. It's the first move only a secure man can make.

  • Name your part before you name his.

The man who apologizes first isn't the one who's most wrong. He's the one who's most free.
