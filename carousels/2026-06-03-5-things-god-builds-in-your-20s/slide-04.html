Kill it. Don't manage it.

Scripture doesn't say work on your sin. It says put it to death.

Your 20s are when you decide: bury it now, or carry it into your 30s.

  • Name the thing. The porn, the laziness, the cowardice.
  • Kill it like it's killing you — because it is.

Don't manage what Christ came to put to death.
