The private screen

Lust doesn’t stay in the tab. It seeps into every room you walk into.

The private screen is where a man practices seeing people as objects. You become what you repeatedly feed yourself — whether you feel it or not.

  • Cut the access, not just the usage. Apps exist for this.
  • Tell one man about it this week. One real conversation beats a hundred private resolutions.

What you feed in the dark shapes who you are in the light.
