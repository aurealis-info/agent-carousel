Thing 3

The cowardice you call patience

You haven’t started it. Haven’t had the conversation. Haven’t made the move you know God is calling you to make.

You call it waiting on God. But there’s a difference between trusting God’s timing and using God as cover for not acting. Fear looks like faith from the outside.

  • Name the specific thing you’ve been avoiding. Don’t let it stay vague.
  • The courage comes in the act, not before it. You won’t feel ready first.

“Waiting on God” and hiding from the call are not the same thing.
