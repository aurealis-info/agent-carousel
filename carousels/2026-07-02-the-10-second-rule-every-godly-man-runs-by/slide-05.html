Step 4

Spend the ten seconds on obedience

A pause with nothing in it just delays the impulse. Fill it with one small act of obedience instead.

Put the phone face down. Close the tab. Send the honest text, not the hot one. That's the rule working.

  • "Whoever is slow to anger is better than the mighty." — Proverbs 16:32
  • A man who rules his own spirit is stronger than one who takes a city.

Ten seconds, run a thousand times, is how a man builds self-control.
