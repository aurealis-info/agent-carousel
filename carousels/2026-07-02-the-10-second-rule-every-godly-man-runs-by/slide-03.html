Step 2

Take the ten seconds on purpose

Not willpower. A rule you set before the moment shows up, so you're not negotiating with yourself in the heat of it.

Ten seconds of silence, or one line of prayer, before your hand moves. That's the whole mechanism.

  • "Lead me not into temptation" takes about three seconds to pray.
  • Say it slow. Let the other seven do their work.

The pause is where God gets a word in.
