Step 1

Name what's actually driving

The text, the tab, the third plate, the reply you want to send hot. None of it is really about the thing in front of you.

It's anger, boredom, loneliness, or pride wearing a costume. You can't rule what you won't name.

  • Before you act, say out loud what you're actually feeling.
  • "I'm not hungry, I'm anxious" changes what happens next.

You can't kill an impulse you refuse to name.
