Step 3

Ask who this actually serves

One question, asked in the pause: does this serve me right now, or the man I'm becoming?

Most impulses only survive the first question. They can't survive the second.

  • If the honest answer is "just me, right now," that's your answer.
  • Self-control isn't repression. It's picking who's actually in charge.

An impulse that has to answer for itself usually loses.
