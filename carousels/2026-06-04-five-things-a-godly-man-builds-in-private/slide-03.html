Discipline 2

A body he can respect

Not vanity.

A man who can’t govern his body can’t govern much else. The gym, the sleep, the food — these aren’t self-worship. They’re stewardship.

  • Show up to train before you feel like it.
  • Sleep like you have somewhere to be tomorrow.

“Present your bodies as a living sacrifice.” — Romans 12:1.
