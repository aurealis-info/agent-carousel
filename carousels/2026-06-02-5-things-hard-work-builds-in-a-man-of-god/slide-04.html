Truth 3

Patience that kills entitlement

Hard work forces you onto God's timeline, not yours. You can't demand fruit before the roots go deep.

The grinding season that feels like wasted time is often the most important formation you'll ever have. God is more interested in who you're becoming than how fast you're arriving.

"Whatever you do, work heartily, as for the Lord and not for men." — Colossians 3:23

God isn't in a hurry. The work isn't either.
