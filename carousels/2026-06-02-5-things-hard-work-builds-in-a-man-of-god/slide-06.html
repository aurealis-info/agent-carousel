Truth 5

Capacity for what's next

God doesn't hand a man a kingdom he hasn't been trained for.

Joseph ran Potiphar's household for years before he ran Egypt. He didn't wait for the big stage — he was faithful on the small one. The grinding season was the training, not the waiting room.

What God is planning to put in your hands requires the man you're becoming right now.

You can't skip the formation and still carry the weight.
