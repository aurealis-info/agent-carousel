A name that outlasts

"A man skilled in his work will serve before kings." — Proverbs 22:29

Not followers. Not a highlight reel. The kind of reputation that's built in private and still standing in public ten years from now.

  • Skill and faithfulness outlast every cultural moment.
  • The algorithm fades. The work you did right doesn't.

The world calls it success. Scripture calls it faithfulness.
