Truth 1

The character you can't fake

Hard work exposes you.

Not the version you'd pitch to a room — the actual one. The man who grinds when it's boring. Who does it right when no one would know if he didn't.

You can fake character in a conversation. You can't fake it across two years of unglamorous work.

What you do when no one's watching is who you are.
