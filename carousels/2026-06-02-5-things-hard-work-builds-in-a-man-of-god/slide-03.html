Truth 2

Authority over your own life

Self-mastery isn't a trait you're given. It's a muscle you train by showing up when it costs you.

Every time you do the hard thing when you don't feel like it, you vote for the man you're becoming. Every time you skip it, you vote the other way.

A man who can govern himself in a boring Tuesday can be trusted with everything else.

You govern yourself on Tuesdays or not at all.
