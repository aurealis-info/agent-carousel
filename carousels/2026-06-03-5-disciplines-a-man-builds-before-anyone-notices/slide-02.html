Discipline 1

Show up before you feel ready

There's no version of the man you want to be that hit snooze until it happened.

The morning is a test. Govern it before the day governs you.

  • Same alarm, seven days a week. No negotiating with yourself at 5 AM.
  • First ten minutes: no phone. Let the day be set before the noise starts.

A man who owns his mornings owns more than that.
