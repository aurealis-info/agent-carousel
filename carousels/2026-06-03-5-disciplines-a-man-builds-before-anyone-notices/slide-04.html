Discipline 3

Address the thing you keep avoiding

There's an apology you owe. A conversation you've pushed to "later" so many times it's gone cold.

A man of integrity doesn't let it fester. He walks toward the hard thing, not away from it.

  • Who do you owe a conversation this week?
  • The longer you wait, the harder it gets — and the smaller you become.

Going first isn't weakness. It's what leaders do.
