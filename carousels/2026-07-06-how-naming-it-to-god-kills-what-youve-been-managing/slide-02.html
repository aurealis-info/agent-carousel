Step 1

Call it what God calls it

"Managing" is the polite word. Scripture just says sin.

Romans 8:13 doesn't say wrestle it into balance. It says put it to death.

  • Write down the actual word for it tonight, not the vague one.
  • Not "a struggle." Sin. Say it that plainly, at least to yourself.

You can't kill a thing you won't name.
