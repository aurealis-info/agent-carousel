Step 3

Pray the kill, not the compromise

Most guys pray for the temptation to ease up a little.

Pray instead that God finishes what He started, all the way through.

  • Ask for death to it, not just distance from it.
  • Name the hour, the app, the scene. Let the prayer be specific.

A softer prayer gets you a softer sin, not a dead one.
