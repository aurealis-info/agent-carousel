Step 1

Name it before you manage it

You can't kill what you won't name. "Struggle" is a word for something comfortable. The porn, the bitterness, the 1 a.m. scroll — name it the way Scripture does.

Then you're talking about a real thing you can actually bring to God, not a vague thing you're politely maintaining.

  • What you name, you can confess.
  • What you confess starts to die.
