Step 4

Run back to the Word, not a plan

Plans fail. Resolve fades by Thursday. But a man anchored in Scripture has something that outlasts his own willpower.

Open it on the day you're strong. Open it on the day you're not. "Submit to God. Resist the devil, and he will flee." James 4:7. That's the whole strategy.

God finishes what He starts in the man who keeps showing up.
