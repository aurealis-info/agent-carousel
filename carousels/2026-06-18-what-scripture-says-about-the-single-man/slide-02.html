Step 1

Paul called it a gift, not a waiting room

1 Corinthians 7:7. Paul called singleness a charism — a grace-gift from God. Not a consolation prize, not a problem to fix, not a gap between now and real life.

A tool God hands a man for a season so he can give himself to one thing: growing in Christ. The guy treating this like a holding pattern is missing what it's actually for.

  • Read 1 Corinthians 7:32–35 tonight. Take it seriously.
  • This is one of the most undivided seasons a man will ever have.

Stop waiting for life to start. This is life.
