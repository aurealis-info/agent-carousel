Step 2

The guy chasing God doesn't smell desperate

She can tell when a man is chasing her to fill something God was supposed to fill. It's a weight she was never meant to carry, and she pulls away from it instinctively.

But a man who's genuinely walking with God — growing, not performing — draws the right woman without strategizing for it.

  • The prayer isn't "give me a wife." It's "make me the man worth marrying."
  • Who you're becoming matters more than when she shows up.

Stop chasing what you need to walk toward.
