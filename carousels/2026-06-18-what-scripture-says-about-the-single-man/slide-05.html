Step 4

Christ completes him. Not her.

The whole man was never meant to wait to arrive. Colossians 2:10 — "You are complete in him." Not when you're married. Not when you're successful. Now.

The man who understands this stops outsourcing his wholeness to the next relationship. He builds his life on something that holds — and becomes someone worth building with.

  • Build your life on Christ, not around the gap.
  • A man at rest in his faith is the most grounded version of himself.

You are already complete. Now go become the man God's building.
