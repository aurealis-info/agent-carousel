Discipline 1

A war with the scroll

His grandfather’s temptation needed a second location. Yours lives in your pocket.

The phone is where lust waits, where comparison breeds, where hours vanish and nothing grows.

Most men don’t declare war. They manage it. A man walking with God doesn’t manage it — he fights.

  • Block one hour a day where the phone doesn’t exist. Non-negotiable.
  • Notice what you pick it up for. Ask if that’s the man you’re becoming.

You can’t govern your world if your phone governs you.
