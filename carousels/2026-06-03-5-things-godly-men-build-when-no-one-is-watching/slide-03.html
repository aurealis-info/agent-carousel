Discipline 2

A body that works

Not a body for Instagram. One that shows up.

The reps nobody films. The mornings you drag yourself out when nothing in you wants to move.

Paul told Timothy to train his body — he knew an undisciplined body and an undisciplined soul tend to run together.

  • Commit to something physical and don’t quit when it stops being interesting.
  • Train it like it’s connected to your character — because it is.

The gym isn’t vanity if what you’re building is virtue.
