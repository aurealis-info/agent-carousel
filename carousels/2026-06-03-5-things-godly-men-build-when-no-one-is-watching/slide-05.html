Discipline 4

A circle that knows your sin

Not a group chat. Not a podcast community. Not your 400 followers.

Two or three men who have your number, know what you’re actually fighting, and will call — not to shame you, but to walk with you.

“Confess your sins to one another, that you may be healed.” — James 5:16.

  • Name one man who knows your real battle right now.
  • If you can’t name him, that’s the gap.

You can’t heal in private what God designed for community.
