Discipline 3

A name people trust

Not a big name. A reliable one.

In your 20s, that trust is built in unglamorous hours — the quality you held when cutting corners was easy, the early you stayed when no one asked, the project no one photographed.

That’s the measure. And it compounds.

  • Do your current job like it’s the one you’re angling for.
  • Be the man people call when it actually matters.

Faithfulness in small things is the audition for bigger ones.
