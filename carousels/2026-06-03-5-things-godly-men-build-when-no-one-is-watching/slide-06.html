Discipline 5

The hour before the world

This is the root. Everything else grows from it or not.

The man who meets God before his phone, his boss, or his anxiety does has already decided who’s in charge of his day.

Twenty minutes. One verse. Actual prayer — not a habit-streak, the real thing. Bring the actual fight, not the Sunday version of it.

  • Open Scripture before you open anything else.
  • Ask God for the day — then go build it.

The man who prays first is ready for the rest.
