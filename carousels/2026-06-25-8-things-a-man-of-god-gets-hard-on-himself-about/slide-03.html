  • 5. What his money says about him when no one's asking.

    The tithe. The giving. The spending. The honest number, not the one for appearances.

  • 6. Whether his anger bleeds into the rest of the day.

    He's allowed to feel it. He's not allowed to take it out on the next room. The man who can feel it without becoming it — that's the one.

  • 7. Where his time actually goes.

    The hours are the same for everyone. What's running his?

  • 8. Whether he's the same man in prayer that he is everywhere else.

    The prayer that's honest is the one that changes him. The performance just uses up minutes.
