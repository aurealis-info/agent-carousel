Let her be drawn, not convinced.

You shouldn't have to persuade her you're a good man. If you're living it, she can see it. Convincing her is the job of a salesman. Being the man is a different thing entirely.

"He who finds a wife finds a good thing." — Proverbs 18:22. The word is finds. Not chases down. Not performs for. The man who's walking the right road — she's somewhere on it too.
