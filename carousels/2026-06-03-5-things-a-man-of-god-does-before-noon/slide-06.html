Step 5

Move your body

Discipline is connected. A man who trains his body learns to govern himself everywhere else.

It doesn't take much — 20 minutes of intentional movement changes what you're capable of mentally. And the man who can't make himself train usually can't make himself do much else either.

  • Twenty minutes. Every day. Non-negotiable.
  • Not for the physique — for the self-government.

A man who trains his body is learning to train his soul.
