Step 4

Guard the first two hours

This is the window before the world starts making demands. Guard it like it belongs to you — because it does.

No Instagram, no group chats, no news until you've done the work. The man who enters his day before the chaos has already won something.

  • No social media until the first block of work is done.
  • You can give the world the second hour. Not the first.

A man who lets the world in first never fully shows up.
