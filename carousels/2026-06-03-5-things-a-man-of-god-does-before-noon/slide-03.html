Step 2

Read the Word before you read the feed

Podcasts, reels, group chats — most of it isn't bad.

But a man who opens Scripture first thinks from a different place all day. The Word reorients you. The feed just redirects your anxiety.

  • Five verses, slowly. Not a Bible plan — a conversation.
  • Ask one thing as you read: "What is this showing me?"

What you read first shapes what you reach for all day.
