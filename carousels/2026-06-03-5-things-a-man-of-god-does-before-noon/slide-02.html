Step 1

Pray before you pick up the phone

The battle starts when the alarm goes off, not when the hard thing hits.

If the first thing you reach for is your phone, you've handed the first hour to the algorithm.

Two minutes of silence before the noise changes what your mind reaches for all day.

  • Don't pick up your phone until you've spoken to God.
  • One sentence is enough: "What do You want from me today?"

The man who prays first builds a day the world can't own.
