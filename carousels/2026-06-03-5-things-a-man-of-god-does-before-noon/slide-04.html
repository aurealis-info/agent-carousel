Step 3

Do the hard thing first

Willpower burns down across the day. By 3 PM, most men are running on fumes.

The task you've been avoiding, the workout, the hard conversation — front-load it. Do it before the day costs you anything.

  • Name one thing this morning you'd rather skip. That's the thing.
  • Do it before you check anything else.

The man who acts before he feels like it is the man who gets things done.
