Step 1

The model was never "I'm in charge"

Leadership in Scripture looks nothing like the world's version.

The pattern is a towel and a basin. A servant before the supper. A man washing feet before he broke bread.

Jesus didn't lead by rank. He led by going first into the cost — and then asking others to follow.

  • Who you lead is determined by who you're willing to serve.

Authority earned through service outlasts authority claimed by position.
