First into the cost

Real authority goes where it asks others to go — before they do.

The hard conversation. The apology. The 6 AM. The thing that costs dignity.

If you're always the last one to absorb a hit, you're not leading. You're directing traffic.

  • Own what went wrong before anyone else does.
  • Take the hit first. Then ask for help.
