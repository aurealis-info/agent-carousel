Step 4

Lead without needing to be seen

The best leadership often leaves no spotlight on the leader. The credit belongs to the room. The win belongs to the people.

The man who needs recognition to lead can only lead when the crowd is watching. But the man who leads whether he's seen or not — he's built on something real.

  • Ask yourself: would I do this if no one ever found out?

The cross was not performed for an audience. Neither is real leadership.
