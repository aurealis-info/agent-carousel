Shift 2

Your days get ordered

Not as duty. As conversation.

You open the Word in the morning because you need it — not to impress anyone. And slowly it starts organizing everything: your money, your mornings, who gets your hours.

“Seek first his kingdom and his righteousness, and all these things will be given to you as well.” — Matthew 6:33

The ordered life is the attractive life.
