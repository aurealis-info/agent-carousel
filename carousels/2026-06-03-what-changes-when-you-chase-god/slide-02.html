Shift 1

The hunger quiets

The desperate man leaks. He shape-shifts to be whatever she might want. He texts first every time just to quiet the ache.

He needs her answer to feel like a man. And she can tell — even when he can’t.

  • When God is the pursuit, the hunger gets fed somewhere real.
  • You stop performing. You start showing up settled.

A man who’s found in God doesn’t need to be found by her.
