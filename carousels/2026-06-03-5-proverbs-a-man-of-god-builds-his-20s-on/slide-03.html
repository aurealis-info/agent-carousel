Verse 2

Skill built in private shows up in public

“Do you see someone skilled in their work? They will serve before kings.” — Prov. 22:29

Excellent work from a man of God is a testimony before he says a word. Mediocre work from a Christian is a bad witness.

  • Do your best work, especially when no one’s watching.
  • Be the man people call when it has to be done right.

The man building in obscurity isn’t being overlooked. He’s being prepared.
