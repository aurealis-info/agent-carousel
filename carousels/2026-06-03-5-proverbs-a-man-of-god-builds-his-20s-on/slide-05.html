Verse 4

Love correction or stay small

“Whoever loves discipline loves knowledge, but whoever hates correction is stupid.” — Prov. 12:1

Solomon didn’t hedge it. The man who deflects every hard truth stays exactly where he is — and calls it the world’s fault.

  • Find one man who’ll tell you the truth without softening it.
  • Absorb it. Don’t defend. Don’t deflect.

Pride resists. Wisdom absorbs.
