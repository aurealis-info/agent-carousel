Verse 1

Stop waiting to feel ready

The man in Proverbs 6 doesn’t hate work. He just keeps waiting for a better moment.

“A little sleep, a little slumber, a little folding of the hands…” — and the years go quietly. — Prov. 6:10

  • Name what you’ve been putting off.
  • Move before you talk yourself back to sleep.

You don’t feel your way into discipline. You act your way into it.
