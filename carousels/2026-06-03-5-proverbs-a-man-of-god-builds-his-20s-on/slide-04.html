Verse 3

Plan. Then hold it loosely.

“In their hearts humans plan their course, but the Lord establishes their steps.” — Prov. 16:9

A godly man plans. He sets goals, builds systems, works with intention. Then he surrenders the outcome. Planning and surrender aren’t opposites — they’re the same discipline at different scales.

  • Work like it depends on you.
  • Pray like it depends on God.

Ambition under God’s hand is one of the most powerful things in the world.
