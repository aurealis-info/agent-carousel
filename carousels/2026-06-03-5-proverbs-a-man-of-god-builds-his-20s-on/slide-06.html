Verse 5

You can’t sharpen yourself

“As iron sharpens iron, so one man sharpens another.” — Prov. 27:17

You can’t get sharper alone. The man who isolates dulls what’s already blunt — and calls it independence.

  • Two or three men who know your sin and your calling.
  • Not a group chat. Real accountability, eye to eye.

The man going somewhere needs men who know where he’s been.
