  1. 6. When you get exactly what you wanted.

    Success reveals more than failure. A man who carries a win humbly is rarer than one who carries a loss.

  2. 7. When you're wrong and someone's watching.

    The apology you make in front of another man is the real one. A private "my bad" costs nothing.

  3. 8. When it's someone else's turn to win.

    Envy is loudest at someone else's blessing. Watch your first reaction — it tells the truth.

  4. 9. When you're in physical pain.

    Suffering strips the performance. Who you are when you're suffering is who you are.
