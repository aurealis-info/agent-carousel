Mark 1

Govern your tongue

The first thing that betrays a man is what comes out of his mouth — the snap, the cut, the thing said before he thought.

"Whoever keeps his mouth and his tongue keeps himself out of trouble." — Proverbs 21:23

  • Notice the 3 seconds before you respond.
  • Ask: would you say this if Christ were in the room?

The governed man pauses. The ungoverned man answers.
