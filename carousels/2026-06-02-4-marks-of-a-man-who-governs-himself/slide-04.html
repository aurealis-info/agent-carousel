Mark 3

Decide before the moment

The man who decides when he's already tempted has already lost. The moment he's in the DMs, in the bar, at 1 AM — the decision was due yesterday.

A governed man makes his calls in the daylight, when he's thinking clearly, before the pressure shows up.

  • Name your limits now, not when you're being tested.
  • Structure protects you when willpower won't.

A man with no decided limits has unlimited exposure.
