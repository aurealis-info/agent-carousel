Mark 4

Submit the battle

Self-control isn't something you crank up. It's a fruit — Galatians 5:23 lists it alongside love and peace as what the Spirit produces in a man.

You pursue the Spirit. The Spirit builds the governance.

  • Discipline alone runs out of gas. The Spirit doesn't.
  • Ask God for governance, not just more strength.

You fight. But you don't fight alone.
