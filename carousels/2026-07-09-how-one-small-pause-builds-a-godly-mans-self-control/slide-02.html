Name the gap before you need it

Every failure you regret had a gap in it. Between the text and the send. The scroll and the search bar. The first drink and the fourth.

You never fell straight into it. You walked through a gap you didn't notice was there.

  • Find your gap on a quiet day, not in the middle of it.

You can't govern a moment you never saw coming.
