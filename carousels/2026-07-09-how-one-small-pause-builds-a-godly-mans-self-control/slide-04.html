Step 3

Practice the pause on small things

You won't out-run a 2 a.m. impulse if you've never told yourself no over lunch.

Self-control is a muscle before it's a moment. Skip the extra scroll. Wait the extra minute. Let the small reps build the gap you'll need later.

  • Practice on the low-stakes version this week.

The rep that feels pointless is the one that saves you later.
