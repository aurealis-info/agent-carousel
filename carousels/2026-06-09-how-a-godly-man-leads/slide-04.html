Step 3

Be the first to serve.

Before the meeting. In the room nobody sees. On the shift nobody wants. Service isn't beneath the leader — for a man following Christ, it's the definition of it.

"The greatest among you shall be your servant." — Matthew 23:11.

  • Find the thing nobody wants to do and do it first.
  • Let who you serve in private tell you who you actually are.

The man who serves in secret leads in public by default.
