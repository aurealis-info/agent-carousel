Step 3

Absorbs what others shouldn't carry

A godly man takes the hit so the people he leads don't have to. He doesn't pass the blame down. He doesn't make the people around him smaller so he can look bigger.

The buck stops here isn't a policy. It's a posture.

  • Don't hand your frustration to your family.
  • The hit stops with you. That's the job.

He absorbs the cost. That's what leaders do.
