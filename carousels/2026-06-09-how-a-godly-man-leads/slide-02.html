Step 1

The cross was a leadership act.

He didn't wait for the disciples to risk first. He went ahead — served before He asked anything, gave before He received anything. Leadership in Christ's image doesn't mean managing people. It means going first into whatever it costs.

  • If Christ went first, you go first.
  • The title is irrelevant. The move is everything.

Every real leader has a cross somewhere in their job description.
