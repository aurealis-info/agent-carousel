Step 1

Goes first into the cost

Real leadership has nothing to do with a title.

It's about who moves first — into the apology, the hard conversation, the work nobody asked for.

The man who waits to be recognized before he leads usually waits his whole life.

  • Don't wait for permission to go first.
  • Start paying the cost before anyone's watching.

The man who goes first is usually the man people follow.
