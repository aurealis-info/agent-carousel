Step 4

Lead hardest when no one's watching.

The real test of leadership isn't a crisis. It's a Tuesday. The habits you hold when there's no audience. The decision you make when it has no witnesses.

Private consistency is what makes public leadership credible. You can't fake what nobody sees — and you can't manufacture what you never built alone.

  • What you are in secret, you are.
  • Everything else is performance.

The leader men follow is the man they can see all the way through.
