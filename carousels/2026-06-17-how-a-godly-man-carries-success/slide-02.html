Step 1

Stay tethered to God, not the results

The win didn't make you. Don't let it reshape you.

The same man who showed up Monday when nothing was happening needs to show up now. Same habits. Same posture before God. Same honesty in the morning.

Continuity is the test of character. Gratitude is the first sign you're passing it.

What changed was your circumstances. Not who you belong to.
