Step 4

Hold it open-handed

What God gives, God can move. Grip it and you're already ruled by fear of losing it.

Held loose, success is a gift and a responsibility. Held tight, it's an idol. The difference isn't what you have. It's how you hold it.

"Humble yourselves before the Lord, and he will lift you up." — James 4:10. The sequence goes that direction, not the reverse.

Hold it loose enough for God to move it.
