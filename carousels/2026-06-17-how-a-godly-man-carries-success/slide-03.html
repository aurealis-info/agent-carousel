Step 2

Keep the men who knew you before

Acclaim is a solvent. It thins the room around you fast.

The men who saw you before the recognition are the ones who can still see you clearly. Don't upgrade your circle when you upgrade your season.

Keep the ones who'd tell you when you're starting to drift. Those men are not optional.

  • One man who knew you before has more value than ten new ones who only know the current version.
