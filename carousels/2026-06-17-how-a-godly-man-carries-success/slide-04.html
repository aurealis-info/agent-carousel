Step 3

Use it to serve, not to signal

A man walking with God doesn't collect wins. He trades them.

Whatever room success opens, the first question isn't "what does this mean for me?" It's "who in this room needs what I now have access to?"

The platform is borrowed. Posture toward it like it belongs to someone else, because it does.

Stewardship, not ownership. That's the posture.
