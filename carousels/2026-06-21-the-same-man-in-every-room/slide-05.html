Step 4

Consistency is what trust is built on

Men follow a man whose private life matches his public one. You cannot lead a room you are lying to.

A man of integrity isn't trying to impress you. He's the same whether you're watching or not. That is what makes him safe to follow.

"Whoever can be trusted with very little can also be trusted with much." Luke 16:10
