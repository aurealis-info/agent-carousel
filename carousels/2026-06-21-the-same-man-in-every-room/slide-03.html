Step 2

It's not about perfection

Integrity doesn't mean you never fall. It means the version of you that falls is the same man who gets back up.

You confess to the same men who saw you at your best. You're not managing a reputation. You're building a character.

  • The confessed life is a clean life.
  • The hidden life is the life that owns you.
