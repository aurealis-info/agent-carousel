What winning actually looks like.

Winning doesn't mean you've mastered it. It means you've tilted the field. The phone is in a drawer. You read first. You know what you're fighting it for. And when you pick it up now, you're choosing to — not being pulled.

"Whatever is true, whatever is honorable, whatever is pure… think about these things." — Philippians 4:8. The mind you feed is the man you become.
