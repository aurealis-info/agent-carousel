Verse 3

Passivity is not peace.

2 Thessalonians 3:10 — “If anyone is not willing to work, let him not eat.”

Paul wrote that to a church. Not a corporation.

Sitting in a “season” when God told you to get up isn’t faith. It’s avoidance wearing a spiritual costume.

Peace isn’t the absence of effort. It’s effort freely given.
