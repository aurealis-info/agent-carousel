Discipline 3

Confess to a real man

Journaling is good. A brother who can look you in the eye heals.

The sin you keep private keeps you. The one you name out loud loses its grip.

  • One guy. His number in your phone.
  • Name the actual thing. Out loud.

"Confess your sins to one another, that you may be healed." — James 5:16
