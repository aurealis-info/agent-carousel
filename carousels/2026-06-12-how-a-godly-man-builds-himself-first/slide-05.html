Step 4

Trust God's timeline, not your hunger

Anxiety about the when is a trust problem. "Delight yourself in the Lord, and he will give you the desires of your heart." — Psalm 37:4.

The desire isn't wrong. The grip on the timeline is.

  • Trust his pace more than your hunger.
  • The right woman recognizes a man at peace with God.

The man God has prepared will find the woman God has prepared.
