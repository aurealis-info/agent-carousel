Step 2

Build your prayer life before your dating life

The man who can't sustain 15 minutes alone with God doesn't have much to lead anyone toward.

Spiritual leadership flows from spiritual life. If it's empty in private, it will be empty when it counts.

  • Daily time in the Word — not when convenient.
  • The man who kneels alone is the man she can follow.

You can't lead her somewhere you've never been.
