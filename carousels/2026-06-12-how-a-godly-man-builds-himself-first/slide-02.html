Step 1

Kill the desperate edge

Desperation is visible. When a man is chasing a wife more than he's chasing God, she can feel it across a coffee table.

The desire is good. The hunger that runs ahead of your character is the problem.

  • A man who needs a relationship to feel whole will take the wrong one.
  • Fix the hunger, not the absence.

You're not ready for her until you're okay without her.
