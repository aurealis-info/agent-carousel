Step 4

Let God build the name

You can’t engineer a reputation God’s supposed to build.

“A man’s gift makes room for him.” — Proverbs 18:16. That’s not passive. It’s do the work and get out of the way.

  • Stop managing your image. Manage your character.

The man God builds lasts. The man you built yourself doesn’t.
