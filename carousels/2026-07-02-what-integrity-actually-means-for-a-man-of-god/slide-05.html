Step 4

It's confessing before you're caught

The man with three versions of himself needs no one to find out. The whole man doesn't wait to be found out.

He brings the split into the light himself, to one honest brother, before it costs him more than his pride.

  • "Confess your sins to one another, that you may be healed." — James 5:16

Integrity isn't never splitting. It's refusing to stay split.
