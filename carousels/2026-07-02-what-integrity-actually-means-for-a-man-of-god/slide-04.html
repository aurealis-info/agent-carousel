Step 3

It's what you do when no one's grading you

Integrity comes from the same root as "integer," a whole number, not split into parts.

You find out if you're whole by watching what happens in the room with no witnesses. The empty apartment. The tab no one's checking.

  • Job made a covenant with his eyes before anyone was watching him watch.

Character is who you are when the audience leaves.
