Step 2

It's the same guy in the group chat and at church

You know the guy with three settings. Reverent on Sunday, crude in the group chat, careful around his girlfriend's dad.

None of those is fake exactly. That's the problem. They're all really him, and none of them is all of him.

  • Ask a friend from each room what you're like there.
  • If the answers don't match, you've found the split.

A divided man is exhausting to be. It's also exhausting to trust.
