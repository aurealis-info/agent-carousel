Step 2

God isn't asking you to wait — he's asking you to build

The years you spend frustrated in the search are the years you could be getting built.

The man God is forming right now — in discipline, in the Word, in real brotherhood — is the man she'll one day be drawn to.

You can't shortcut formation.

  • "He who finds a wife finds a good thing." — Proverbs 18:22. Note the verb. He finds.
  • You don't manufacture it. You become the kind of man who can build something real.

The search isn't the work. Becoming is.
