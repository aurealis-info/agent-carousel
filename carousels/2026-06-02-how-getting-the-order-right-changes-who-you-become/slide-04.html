Step 3

Chasing God looks different than you think

It isn't just quiet times and Sunday attendance.

It's killing the porn. Showing up for your brothers when you're empty. Doing the job right when no one is watching.

That's formation — not feelings, not experiences. Faithfulness in private.

  • The hidden fidelity is what shapes the visible man.
  • Every act of private obedience builds the version of you she'll one day choose.

The man you're becoming in secret is the man who shows up everywhere else.
