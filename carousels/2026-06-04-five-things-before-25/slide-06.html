Discipline 5

Become a man people can count on

Show up when you said you would. Finish what you started. Do the hard thing without being asked twice.

The 20s are where a man learns whether he's reliable — whether he can be trusted with small things before God gives him bigger ones.

  • Be the guy who shows up early and stays until it's done.
  • Your word is your character. Build it now, while it's still cheap.

Work isn't the curse. Pointless work is. Find something worth building and build it right.
