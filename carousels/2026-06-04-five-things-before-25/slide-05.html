Discipline 4

Find one man who knows

Not a small group. Not Discord. Not a podcast.

One man. Face to face. Who knows exactly what you're fighting and is allowed to ask the question you'd rather not answer.

  • Find the guy who'll pick up the phone at midnight.
  • James 5:16 isn't optional — it's the mechanism that heals.

The man who confesses to no one carries the weight alone.
