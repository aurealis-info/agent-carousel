Discipline 3

Use your body on purpose

You don't have to be an athlete. You do have to treat the body like something worth stewarding.

The man who never does anything physically hard loses a tolerance for discomfort — and it shows up everywhere else.

  • Three mornings a week of something that costs you. Not for looks.
  • Hard reps train the mind to push through hard moments later.

A man who governs his body can govern almost anything else.
