Discipline 1

Build a morning that's yours

The man who owns the first hour owns the day.

Not checking the phone before you've prayed. Not starting tired because you stayed up watching nothing.

Up before it's required. Word before the world gets in.

  • 20 minutes in Scripture before anything else gets your attention.
  • Quiet before the noise. This is not about productivity — it's about who speaks to you first.

The morning is the one hour the phone can't compete with — if you choose it.
