Discipline 2

Put your sin to death

Not "I'm working on it." Not "it's a season."

Colossians 3:5 says put it to death. The porn, the hours of scrolling, whatever's leaking your strength.

The man who manages his sin stays stuck in it. The man who kills it walks free.

  • Name it out loud — to yourself first, then to one man.
  • Make a plan, not a promise. Promises break. Plans have steps.

Scripture doesn't say manage your sin. It says mortify it.
