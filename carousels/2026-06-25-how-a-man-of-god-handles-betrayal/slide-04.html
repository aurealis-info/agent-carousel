Step 3

Name the wound once. Then stop.

There's a difference between naming what happened and rehearsing it.

Tell one man the real story. The full version. Once. Then stop talking about it.

Rehearsing a wound turns it into a wall. You stop processing and start building a case.

  • One honest conversation with one trusted man.
  • After that — let it rest.

What you keep replaying keeps running you.
