Step 4

Forgive before it makes sense

You forgive because Christ forgave you when you were still in the wrong. Not because they deserve it. Not because it doesn't still hurt.

Forgiveness isn't reconciliation. You don't have to restore the position. But you release the debt.

  • The man chained to his wound is chained to the man who wounded him.
  • That's not strength. That's a cage.

"Forgiving one another, as God in Christ forgave you." — Ephesians 4:32
