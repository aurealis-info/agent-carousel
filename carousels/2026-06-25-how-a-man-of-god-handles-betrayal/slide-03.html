Step 2

Grieve it. Don't perform it.

You don't have to be okay. You're allowed to be hurt.

But take the wound to God before you take it to the group chat. He already knows what it cost you. Tell Him the honest version.

  • Sit with it in prayer. Name the pain exactly.
  • Then stop broadcasting it. The group chat won't heal it.

"He heals the brokenhearted and binds up their wounds." — Psalm 147:3
