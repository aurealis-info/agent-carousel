Step 1

Don't move in the first wave

The worst decisions a man makes come from the freshest pain.

The text he wants to send at midnight. The meeting he wants to cancel. The announcement he wants to make.

None of that is a decision yet. That's a reaction.

  • Give it 24 hours before you do anything.
  • Say nothing. Text nothing. Let the wave pass.

The wound is real. The reaction can wait.
