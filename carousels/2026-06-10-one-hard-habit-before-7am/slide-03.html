Hard first, easy after

Put the hard thing at the front — Scripture before the feed, the run before the excuses, the cold shower before the comfort. The brain is fresh, the will is clean, and resistance is at its lowest.

What's hard before 7 AM is easier than you think. What you push to later, you skip.

  • First thing. Hardest thing. Same thing.

The man who does hard things first stops fearing them.
