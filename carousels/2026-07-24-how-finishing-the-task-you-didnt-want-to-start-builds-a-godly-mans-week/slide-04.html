Step 3

Let the small rep count

You don't need the whole project done today. You need today's rep done today.

The man who does that for a year built something. The man waiting to feel ready built nothing.

  • Fifteen honest minutes beats two hours you never start.
  • Track the rep, not the mood.

A year of small reps outbuilds a year of waiting to feel ready.
