Step 2

Start before you feel ready

Motivation shows up after you move, not before.

Colossians 3:23: whatever you do, work at it as for the Lord. That verse doesn't wait for you to feel like it.

  • Open the document before you feel inspired.
  • Let feeling ready be the reward, not the requirement.

The feeling was never coming first. Move, and let it catch up.
