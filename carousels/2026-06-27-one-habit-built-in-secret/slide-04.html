Step 3

Tie it to worship, not willpower

Willpower is a depletable resource. It works for about two weeks.

But a man who reads Scripture because Christ is real doesn't need a streak to keep him going. He goes to the Word because God is there.

The habit that outlasts your feelings is the one rooted in something bigger than how you feel.

  • Connect the rep to the reason.
  • Discipline from faith outlasts discipline from willpower every time.

If the habit dies when your feelings die, it wasn't worship yet.
