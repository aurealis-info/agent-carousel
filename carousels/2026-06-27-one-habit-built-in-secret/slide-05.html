Step 4

Let faithfulness compound

One week of the same faithful thing feels like nothing. One year of it feels like a man.

You don't see compound interest for a long time. Then you see all of it at once.

"Whoever can be trusted with very little can also be trusted with much." — Luke 16:10

  • Pick one thing. Not five.
  • Run it daily for thirty days without skipping.
  • Then add the next one.

Faithfulness in small things is the only preparation for large ones.
