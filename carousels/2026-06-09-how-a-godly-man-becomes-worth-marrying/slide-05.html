Step 4

Let Him Write the Timeline

The man who forces the timeline is the man who marries the wrong season.

Not every woman is the woman. Not every "I'm ready" is actually ready. Loneliness is not a sign. Hunger is not a compass.

Trust that God's timing isn't delay — it's preparation. The wait is doing something in you.

  • Stop mistaking loneliness for urgency.
  • Rest is part of the assignment.

The man worth marrying is built in the years he stopped chasing and started walking.
