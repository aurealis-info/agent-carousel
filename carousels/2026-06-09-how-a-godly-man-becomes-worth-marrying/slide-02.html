Step 1

Stop Auditioning

You can't lead a woman you're performing for.

The man who needs her approval is already handing her the controls — and she knows it before you do.

Most men are building a version of themselves for an audience of one. That version is always a little fake, and fakes don't hold.

  • Drop the version you're showing. Build the man underneath.
  • The goal is character, not impression management.

The guy desperate for a wife sounds desperate. She can tell.
