Step 3

The Work Comes Before the Reward

God gave Adam a garden to tend before He gave him Eve.

The work came first. Not to earn her — to be ready for her. The man who skips the tending stage has nothing to bring into what he's asking God to give him.

  • What have you built that you'd be proud to bring someone into?
  • Your finances, your character, your walk — these are not separate from marriage prep. They are marriage prep.

You become the man before you meet her. Not after.
