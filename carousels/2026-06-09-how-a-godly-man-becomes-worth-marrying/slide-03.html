Step 2

Build the Root First

The fruit — the discipline, the ambition, the gym — is all downstream of the root.

Faith is the root. When a man has that order wrong, he's building a house on sand — looks real until the pressure hits.

A man who walks with God first grows into someone worth walking beside.

  • Ask yourself: am I chasing God, or becoming impressive?
  • The answer tells you everything about where you'll be in five years.

Chase Him first. Everything else follows.
