Step 2

It's a fruit, not a grind

Galatians lists self-control after love, joy, and peace. It grows. You don't manufacture it.

Galatians 5:22-23: the fruit of the Spirit is love, joy, peace, patience... and self-control.

The Spirit builds it in you as you stay close to Him, not as you white-knuckle harder.

  • Ask if you're praying for control or performing it.

You don't grind out a fruit. You stay planted and let it grow.
