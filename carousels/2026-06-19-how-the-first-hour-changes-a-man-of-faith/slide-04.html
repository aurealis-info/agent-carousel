Step 3

Move your body. It's part of the stewardship.

You were given one body for the whole of your life. The discipline of moving it isn't vanity. It's faithfulness.

The man who takes care of his body takes care of what God entrusted to him. Twenty minutes before the day claims everything else. No excuses.

  • Showing up beats showing off.
  • Consistency is the discipline; intensity comes later.
