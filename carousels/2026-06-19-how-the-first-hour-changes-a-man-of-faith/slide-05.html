Step 4

Name one thing you're trusting God with today

Not a list. One thing. The conversation you're dreading, the decision you can't see clearly, the thing that woke you up at 3 a.m.

Bring it to God and then go do the work, trusting He's in the outcome. That's the difference between a man who carries a full day and a man who's crushed by it.

The man who starts with God rarely loses the day.
