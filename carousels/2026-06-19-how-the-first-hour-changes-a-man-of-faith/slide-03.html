Step 2

Pray before you plan

Most men have the order backwards. They plan first, then ask God to bless what they've already decided.

Try the other direction. Bring the day to Him before you build it. He sees things you don't, and the plan that starts in prayer is a different plan than the one that starts with a to-do list.

A plan without prayer is a man working alone.
