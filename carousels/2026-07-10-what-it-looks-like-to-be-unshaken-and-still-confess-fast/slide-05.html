Step 4

Practice the fast apology this week

Pick the smallest thing you're wrong about right now. Not the big one. The small one you keep excusing.

Say it out loud to the person before the day ends.

  • "I was short with you earlier. That was on me." Nine words. Say them.

The fastest way to become unshaken is to stop needing to be seen as flawless.
