Step 2

Confession isn't weakness. Hiding is.

The guy who never admits he's wrong isn't strong. He's terrified of what happens if the room finds out he's human.

A man secure in Christ doesn't need the room to think he's flawless.

  • Fast confession is what confidence in God's grace actually looks like.

The man who can say "I was wrong" fastest is usually the least afraid one in the room.
