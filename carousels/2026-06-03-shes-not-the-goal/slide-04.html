Step 3

The verse men misread

"He who finds a wife finds a good thing." — Proverbs 18:22

Men read that as a mandate to search. The Hebrew word for "find" means to encounter — to come upon.

Not to hunt. To arrive in the right place, being the right man, and come upon something good.

  • You can't manufacture the encounter.
  • You can become the man it happens to.
