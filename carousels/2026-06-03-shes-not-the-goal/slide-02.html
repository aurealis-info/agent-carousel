The pursuit is the problem

They want to feel chosen. To feel less alone. To feel complete.

That's not love — that's need wearing love's face.

  • A woman worth marrying can smell the difference.
  • She's not looking for a man who needs her. She's looking for one who chose her.

Neediness is not pursuit. It's a search for rescue.
