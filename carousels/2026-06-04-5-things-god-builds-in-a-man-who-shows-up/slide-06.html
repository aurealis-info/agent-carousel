Trait 5

Someone worth following

Leadership isn't a title. It's the overflow of who you've become.

The man who does the unglamorous work of building himself — spiritually, physically, in his word — has something to give.

  • Not "do what I say." First to apologize. First to serve. First into the cost.

You can't lead from a life you haven't built.
