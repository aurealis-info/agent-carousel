Close the gap

Church version, gym version, late-night version. If those are three different people, none of them are real. The goal isn't to perform better in public. It's to narrow the distance between who you are privately and who you say you are.

  • What would your closest friend be surprised to learn about you?
  • That gap is exactly where the work is.

One man. One life. The gap is where formation happens.
