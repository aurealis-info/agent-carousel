Step 4

Live like someone is watching — because someone is

Not an audience. Not your accountability partner. God sees the private tab. He sees the version of you that shows up after midnight. He's not shocked by it. And He's not done with it either.

That's not a threat. It's actually the most freeing thing about all of it.

  • The question isn't whether you can get away with it.
  • It's whether this is who you want to become.

A man who lives like that builds something he can stand behind.
