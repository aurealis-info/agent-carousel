What you confess, you begin to outgrow

You don't need to be a different man before you bring it into the light. You bring it into the light as the man you are, and you begin to change from there.

"Everything exposed by the light becomes visible." — Ephesians 5:13

  • What are you keeping in the dark that's keeping you the same?

What you confess starts dying. What you protect keeps living.
