Step 4

Keep the streak, not the feeling

The morning you wake up tired, achy, and don't feel like it — that's the actual rep. Not the morning it comes easy. The hard morning is the one that forms you.

Motivation is a fair-weather friend. Discipline shows up regardless.

Character isn't built in the good mornings. It's built in the ones you don't want.
