A Clean Conscience

Most men spend their 20s managing sin instead of killing it.

That tab. That private version of yourself. It doesn't stay contained — it seeps into how you love, how you lead, how you look in the mirror.

You're still forming. Fix it now, while you're pliable.

  • Name it out loud to one man who'll ask again next week.
  • A confessed thing loses its grip. A hidden thing grows.

Kill it now. The man at 35 will thank you.
