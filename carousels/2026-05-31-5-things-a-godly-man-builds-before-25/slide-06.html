Thing 5

A Skill Worth Paying For

Not a title. Not a degree on a wall. Something useful — something you can do that makes other men's lives easier or better.

The 20s are for reps, not results. The reps are the work. You won't feel ready. Do it anyway.

  • Pick one thing. Put five real years into it.
  • Useful men get called. Passive men wait.

A man nobody can count on built nothing.
