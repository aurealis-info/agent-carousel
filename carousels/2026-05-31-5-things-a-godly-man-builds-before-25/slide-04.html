A Daily Anchor in the Word

Not devotionals. Not podcasts. The Book, in the morning, before your phone tells you who you are.

One chapter. What it actually says. Write one line from it down. Do that for five years.

  • Before the scroll. Every day. No exceptions.
  • The man in the Word for a decade is not the same man.

Five years of Scripture makes you hard to talk out of what you know.
