A Body He Stewards

God gave you a body built for work. Your 20s are the window to build the physical base that carries everything else — work, marriage, war, whatever comes.

Skipping it isn't humility. It's just sloth in a spiritual costume.

  • Show up before you feel like it.
  • Three days a week, for five years, changes a man.

Stewardship isn't vanity. It's faithfulness.
