Step 1

Call it sin, not a struggle

"Struggle" sounds like something happening to you. Sin is something you're doing.

The porn, the 1 AM scroll, the anger you let loose on the people who love you most. Name it plainly before God, not in the softened code you'd use with your small group.

  • Say the actual word in prayer, not the polite one.
  • You can't put to death what you won't name.

A struggle gets managed. A named sin gets killed.
