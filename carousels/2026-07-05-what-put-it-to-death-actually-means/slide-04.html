Step 3

Kill it by the Spirit, not grit

Grit gets you a few clean days. It was never built to carry you past that.

Paul told the Romans the flesh dies by the Spirit's work in a man, not by his own gritted teeth.

  • "If by the Spirit you put to death the deeds of the flesh, you will live." — Romans 8:13
  • Ask God to do in you what your own effort keeps failing to do.

Grit manages sin for a while. The Spirit actually kills it.
