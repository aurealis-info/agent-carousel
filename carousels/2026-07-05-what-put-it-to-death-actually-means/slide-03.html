Step 2

Cut off what feeds it

Willpower alone loses to a phone that's always in your pocket.

Killing sin starts with the boring stuff: the app deleted, the browser locked down, the door you stop walking through at midnight.

  • Make the next sin cost you more effort, not less.
  • A man who guards his eyes first fights half as many battles.

Starve it before you try to outmuscle it.
