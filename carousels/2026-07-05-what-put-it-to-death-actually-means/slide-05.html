Step 4

Say it out loud to one man

Sin you carry alone keeps its strength. Sin you speak starts losing it the moment it leaves your mouth.

Find one brother who already knows your mess. Tell him what you're killing this week, not just what you're "working on."

  • Text him tonight, before you talk yourself out of it.
  • What stays hidden keeps growing. What gets confessed starts dying.

You don't manage what's killing you. You kill it, and you don't do it alone.
