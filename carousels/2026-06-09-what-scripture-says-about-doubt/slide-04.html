Step 3

“Lord, I believe — help my unbelief”

Mark 9:24. A man brings his doubt directly to Jesus — and Jesus heals his son anyway.

That prayer doesn’t clean up the tension. It brings the tension to the right place.

  • The prayer doesn’t have to be clean. It just has to be honest.
  • Bringing doubt to God is not the opposite of faith. It might be the beginning of it.

God honors the man who shows up with questions more than the man who never asks them.
