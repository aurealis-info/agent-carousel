Step 2

Thomas doubted standing in front of the risen Christ

Matthew 28:17 — some saw him and worshipped. And some doubted.

Jesus didn’t send the doubters home. He gave them the mission.

  • God doesn’t wait for your doubt to clear before He works through you.
  • He showed Thomas his hands. He can meet you where you are.

Faith in the presence of doubt is still faith.
