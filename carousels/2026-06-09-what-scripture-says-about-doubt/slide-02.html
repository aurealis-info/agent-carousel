Step 1

Doubt means you’re still paying attention

A man with dead faith doesn’t wrestle with God. He just checks out.

Doubt means the question still matters to you. That’s not weakness — that’s someone who hasn’t given up.

  • Name the doubt instead of burying it.
  • Bring it to God, not the comment section.

Doubt that wrestles with God is closer to faith than certainty that never prays.
