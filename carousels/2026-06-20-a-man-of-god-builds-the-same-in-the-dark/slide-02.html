Step 1

What he does at 6 AM shapes what he reaches for at noon

Most men have a public version and a private one. The public one is polished. The private one runs the show.

The question is never what you do when someone's watching. It's what you do when there's no one to perform for.

  • Open the Word before you open your phone. Even one verse.
  • Do the hard thing before the day asks you to. Don't wait for an audience.

The man who shows up at 6 AM, for God alone, is the man worth trusting at noon.
