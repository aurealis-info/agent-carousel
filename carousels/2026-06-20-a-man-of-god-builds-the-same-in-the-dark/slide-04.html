Step 3

The unglamorous work is the actual work

You're going to build a life from hundreds of days nobody photographs. Tasks nobody thanks you for. Work nobody asks for an update on.

This is not a detour from what God's building. It is what He's building.

  • Finish the thing you promised yourself, even after the motivation is gone.
  • Log nothing. Do it anyway. The record is elsewhere.

The faithfulness no one photographed is what God works with.
