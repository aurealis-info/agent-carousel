Step 4

What you protect in private is what you're known for in public

You can perform integrity once or twice. You cannot perform it across a decade. Eventually what you are in private comes to the surface.

The man walking it quietly is the man who carries it without effort. The one faking it is one crisis away from the truth coming out.

  • Name one thing you've been consistent in where no one's watching.
  • Name one place the private and public versions don't match yet. Work there next.

Build the private man first. The public one shows up on its own.
