Check what's driving you

Before the move — sit with the question: why, really?

Are you running toward something real, or away from something harder?

The heart hides behind good-looking reasons.

"Keep your heart with all diligence, for from it spring the issues of life." — Proverbs 4:23

  • Name the real reason. Then ask if God's in it.

The issue of the move is almost always the issue of the heart.
