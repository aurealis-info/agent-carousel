Slow is faster than hasty

The urgent feeling is a liar.

What feels like "now or never" usually isn't — it's just pressure looking for a release valve.

"The plans of the diligent lead surely to plenty, but everyone who is hasty comes only to poverty." — Proverbs 21:5

  • Is this urgency from God, or from fear?

Patience isn't weakness. It's the long game.
