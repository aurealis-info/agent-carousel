Get real men in the room

Not a podcast. Not a comment thread.

Men who know your patterns, your sin, and your blind spots — and will tell you what you don't want to hear.

"Where there is no counsel, the people fall; but in the multitude of counselors there is safety." — Proverbs 11:14

  • Who in your life can actually talk you out of a bad move?
  • If no one has that access, fix that first.

The lone-wolf decision is a liability.
