Your plan isn't the final plan

You can have a roadmap, a mentor, and a ten-year vision.

God still reserves the right to redirect.

Not to derail you — to direct you.

"A man's heart plans his way, but the Lord establishes his steps." — Proverbs 16:9

  • Submit the plan. Hold the direction loosely.

Your job is to move. His is to route.
