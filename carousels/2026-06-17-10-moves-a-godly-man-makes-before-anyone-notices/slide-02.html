  2. 2. He makes the uncomfortable call no one else wants to make.

    The one everyone's been avoiding. He goes first into the cost.

    Not to feel righteous. Because it's what Christ asked. And because it's hard.

    Not the man who can do something for him. The one no one else is talking to.
