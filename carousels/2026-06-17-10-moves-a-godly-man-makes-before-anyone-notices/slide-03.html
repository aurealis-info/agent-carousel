  1. 6. He keeps his word to himself when no one knows he gave it.

    The man who keeps small private commitments is the one you trust with large public ones.

  2. 7. He tells the truth when a lie would have been easier.

    Not dramatic truth-telling. Just the actual truth, quietly, when it costs him something.

  3. 8. He shows up before he feels ready.

    Most men wait for certainty. He moves in faith and figures it out on the way.

  4. 9. He does the work no one asked him for.

    The report that needed writing. The problem no one assigned. The thing that should exist.

  5. 10. He names God in what went right.

    Not "I worked hard." God was faithful, and I showed up. That's the whole story.
