Trait 1

He credits God out loud

When the opportunity comes — you say God. Not just in private. In the meeting. The dinner. The conversation where it might actually cost you something.

Easy to thank God at church. The trait God looks for is whether you credit Him at the table where it counts.

  • “God opened this” is a different sentence than “I worked hard.”
  • You won’t feel ready to say it. Say it anyway.

He speaks God’s name in the room where it might make things awkward.
