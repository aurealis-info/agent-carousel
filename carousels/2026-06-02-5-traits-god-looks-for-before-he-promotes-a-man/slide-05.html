Trait 4

He stays the same

The promotion. The recognition. The first big win. Watch what happens to a man right after.

If he gets small with the people around him, God noticed. If he gets bigger with them — God noticed that too.

  • Success is a test most men fail quietly.
  • Who you eat with after the win says more than who you prayed with before it.

He’s the same man at the table the morning after.
