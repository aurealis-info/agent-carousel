Trait 5

He kneels last

Most men kneel before the breakthrough. The humble man kneels after.

Before you take the bow, take it back to God. Lay down what He gave you. He doesn’t need your success — He wants your heart.

“Humble yourselves before the Lord, and he will exalt you.” — James 4:10

He doesn’t wait until something breaks to get back on his knees.
