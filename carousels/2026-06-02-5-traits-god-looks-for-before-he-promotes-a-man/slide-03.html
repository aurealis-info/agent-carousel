Trait 2

He listens to older men

You can’t be promoted by a God you only consult yourself about.

Find the older man who has walked where you’re headed. Sit at the table longer than feels natural. Most men stop listening before the real conversation starts.

  • Seek the man who knows your blind spots — and let him in.
  • The counsel you don’t want is usually the counsel you need.

A man who only hears himself out is building on sand.
