Trait 3

He takes the rebuke

Pride doesn’t argue. It goes quiet and pays you back later — through distance, coldness, a slow withdrawal.

A humble man can be corrected and walk back into the same room like it happened.

“Whoever heeds life-giving correction will be at home among the wise.” — Prov. 15:31

If you can’t be corrected, you can’t be trusted.
