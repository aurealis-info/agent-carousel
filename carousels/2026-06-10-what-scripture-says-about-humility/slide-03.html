Step 2

He leads by going first into the cost

A humble man doesn't wait for permission to serve. He goes first.

First to apologize. First to take the hit. First to step into the uncomfortable moment. That's not weakness — it's what it looks like when Christ moves through a man.

  • First to own the mistake.
  • First to absorb the cost so someone else doesn't have to.

Leadership isn't "do what I say." It's "watch what I do."
