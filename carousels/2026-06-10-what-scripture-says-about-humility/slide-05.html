Step 4

The man who can be corrected

If no one in your life can challenge you, it's not because you've arrived.

It's because you've made yourself unreachable. That's a quieter kind of pride — and it's harder to catch.

"Walk humbly with your God." — Micah 6:8. The whole thing is in that line.

The man who can be corrected is the man who keeps becoming.
