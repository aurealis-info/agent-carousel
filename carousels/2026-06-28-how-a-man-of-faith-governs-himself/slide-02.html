Step 1

Check what runs you

The man whose phone decides his morning doesn't run his morning.

Before you lead a team, a relationship, or a family, ask what actually has your obedience right now.

  • What gets your first hour every day?
  • What do you reach for when you're stressed or bored?
  • That's who's running you — and God's watching the answer.

What owns your habits owns you.
