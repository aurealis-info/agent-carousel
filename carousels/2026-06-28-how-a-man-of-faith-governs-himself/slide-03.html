Step 2

Replace motivation with rule

A man of God doesn't wait to feel like praying or training or opening his Bible.

He has a rule. Open the Word before you open your notifications. Move your body before you check the feed. The rule shows up on the days motivation won't.

  • Motivation is a feeling. Rules are a structure.
  • Feelings follow actions — so start the action.

Discipline is what faith looks like when you're tired.
