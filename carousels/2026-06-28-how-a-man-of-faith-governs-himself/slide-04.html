Step 3

Name where you're still weak

The man who won't name his weakness can't fight it. He can only manage it — and managing it keeps it comfortable.

One brother who knows your actual battle is worth more than a hundred who think you have it together.

  • You don't have to announce it to everyone.
  • You have to confess it to someone. James 5:16.
  • What you won't name still runs you.

Exposure breaks what privacy protects.
