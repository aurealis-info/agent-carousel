Step 4

Submit the body to the same Lord

Your schedule, your sleep, your screen, your body — these are not neutral territory. A man who is Christ's submits them, not just manages them.

"I can do all things through Christ who strengthens me." That verse lives in Tuesday mornings, not just sermons. The man who gets that right is harder to ambush.

  • Rested, disciplined men are harder targets for sin.
  • Govern the body and the spirit fights from solid ground.

He governs best who lets himself be governed first.
