Trait 1

He absorbs the cost first

Real leadership isn't "follow me."

It's "I'll take this hit first."

Christ didn't lead from the back of the room. He went to the cross.

  • First to apologize. First to serve at the table.
  • First to show up when there's nothing to gain.

The man who leads is the man who already paid.
