Trait 3

He speaks last

The man who has to fill every silence is the man who's still performing.

Wisdom doesn't fight for airtime. It waits until it's the only thing left in the room.

  • Ask the question. Then stay quiet long enough to actually hear it.
  • The man who listens well is the man people actually trust with the hard thing.

"The one who has knowledge uses words with restraint." — Proverbs 17:27.
