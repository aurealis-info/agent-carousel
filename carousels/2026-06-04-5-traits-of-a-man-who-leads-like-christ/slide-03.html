Trait 2

He leads himself first

The platform is downstream from the character.

What he does at midnight with no one watching qualifies him — or disqualifies him — at noon.

  • A man who can't govern himself has nothing real to offer.
  • Self-mastery isn't the goal. It's the prerequisite.

You don't build a reputation. You build a man. The reputation follows.
