Trait 5

He points past himself

A man leading for his own legacy is just insecurity in charge.

Every man in Scripture God trusted with real authority was pointing people toward God, not toward himself.

  • He's not building a brand. He's building the kingdom.
  • The smaller he gets, the more useful he becomes.

"He must become greater. I must become less." — John 3:30.
