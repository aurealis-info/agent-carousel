Let it follow you into the day.

The verse isn't just for the morning. It's a lens you carry. When the hard thing happens at 2 PM, when the pressure hits, when you're about to react — you'll be surprised what comes back. "Be transformed by the renewing of your mind." — Romans 12:2. This is how that actually works. Not in a moment. Faithfully, over time.
