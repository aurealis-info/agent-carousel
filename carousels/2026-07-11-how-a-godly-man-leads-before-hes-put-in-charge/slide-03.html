Step 2

Serve where no one counts it

"Whoever wants to become great among you must be your servant." (Mark 10:43)

Not the serving that gets seen. The dish nobody saw you wash. The ride nobody knew you gave.

  • Look for the job with no credit attached.
  • Do it anyway, and don't mention it.

Greatness that needs an audience isn't the kind Christ meant.
