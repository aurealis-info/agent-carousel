Step 4

Let God do the promoting

"Humble yourselves before the Lord, and he will lift you up." (James 4:10)

You don't have to campaign for the seat. Do the cost. Let Him decide the timing.

  • Stop angling for the recognition.
  • Keep showing up where it's unglamorous.

The man who goes first into the cost is the one people follow without being told to.
