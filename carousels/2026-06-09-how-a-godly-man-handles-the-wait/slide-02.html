Step 1

Stop Performing for a Future Wife

You've been shaping a version of yourself with her in mind. That's the wrong audience.

The discipline, the early mornings, the work on your character — do it before God, not for her. Formation aimed at a person fades the second she doesn't show up.

  • The man who performs for a person fades when she disappoints him.
  • The man who forms before God carries it everywhere.

Formation before God outlasts performance for a person.
