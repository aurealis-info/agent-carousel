Step 2

The Waiting Season Is a Building Season

Most men treat waiting as a pause — something to survive until the real life starts. It isn't.

It's the only window where you get to become who you are before another person's needs fill the room. The man who wasted that window will feel it later.

  • Build the prayer life now, not after the ring.
  • Kill the sin now, before she inherits it.
  • Find the brotherhood now — she was never designed to be your only person.

You don't find the right person. You become the right person.
