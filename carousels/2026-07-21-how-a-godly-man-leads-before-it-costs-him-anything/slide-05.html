Step 4

He stays when leaving is easier

Christ didn't leave when the cost got real. He went to the cross instead of the exit.

A godly man learns leadership is measured by what he stays for, not what he starts.

  • Name the thing you want to walk away from right now.
  • Stay one more week before you decide.

The man who stays when it's hard is the only one worth following.
