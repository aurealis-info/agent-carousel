Step 3

He tells the truth before he's asked

Waiting to be asked feels safe. It's cowardice with better manners.

A godly man names what everyone's thinking before the room forces it out of him.

  • Say the hard sentence early, not last.
  • Say it to the person, not about them.

Silence he could've broken is a debt he still owes.
