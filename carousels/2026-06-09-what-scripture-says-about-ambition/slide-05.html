Step 4

Build Something Worth Keeping

Your 20s are where a man gets built — not to perform or prove, but to become the kind of man who builds things that last.

A reputation. A family. Work that survives you. Something you can hand to your son without embarrassment.

  • Small faithful work is the foundation of everything else.
  • Shortcuts don't show up in what you leave behind.

Be ambitious. Just make sure the thing you're reaching for is worth having.
