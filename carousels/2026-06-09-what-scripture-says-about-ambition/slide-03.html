Step 2

Work as Worship

Paul doesn't tell men to work to get rich. He tells them to work so they have something to give — and so their lives command respect.

"Whatever you do, work heartily, as for the Lord and not for men." — Colossians 3:23.

  • Your effort in the ordinary is a form of worship.
  • The man who halfhearts it says something about what he believes.

Bring your whole effort. The audience is bigger than your boss.
