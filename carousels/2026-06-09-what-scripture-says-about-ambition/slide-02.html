Step 1

Stewardship, Not Pride

Pride chases the title. Stewardship chases the work. The difference is who the result is for.

Jesus's parable of the talents doesn't reward the man who buried his to stay humble. It rewards the man who multiplied — who took what he was given and built something with it.

  • Don't bury your ability in the name of modesty.
  • Ambition in the right hands builds things that outlast the man.

Burying your talent isn't humility. It's fear dressed up as virtue.
