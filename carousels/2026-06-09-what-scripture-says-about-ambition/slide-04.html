Without God It Becomes Consuming

Ambition detached from God turns into a hunger that eats everything — relationships, integrity, the man himself. Proverbs runs this warning on a loop.

The measure is what you'd sacrifice to get there.

  • If the answer is "anything," you're already lost.
  • What a man chases shapes the man he becomes.

You can want more. Just know why — and who it's for.
