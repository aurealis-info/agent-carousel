Apologize faster than you think you should.

When you've transferred it — and you will — don't wait. Apologize quickly. Specifically. Without defense. Not "I'm sorry you felt that way." Own the actual thing you did. It is not weakness. The man who can do this is the man the people around him will trust with more. A quick, clean apology is one of the most underrated acts of leadership available to you.
