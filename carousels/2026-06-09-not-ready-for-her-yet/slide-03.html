She can smell the need.

A man who needs a wife to feel whole is a man who will ask her to carry what she was never built to carry.

She can tell the difference between a man pursuing her and a man pursuing what she represents. One draws her in. The other wears her out.

  • Stop performing confidence. Start building the real thing.
  • The version of you rooted in Christ doesn't need to convince anyone.

A man who doesn't need her to complete him is a man she can actually choose.
