Step 4

Chase God like you mean it.

"Seek first his kingdom and his righteousness, and all these things will be added to you." — Matthew 6:33.

The promise isn't indifference to your wants. It's a different order. He doesn't say stop wanting. He says stop leading with the wanting.

  • One man walking with God changes every room he enters.
  • Including the one that includes her — when the time is right.

Stop chasing what God is building you toward. Chase Him.
