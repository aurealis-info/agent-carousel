Step 1

The want is real. The timing is not.

The longing for a wife isn't wrong. God put it there.

But the desperate man chases the feeling — the relationship that will finally make him feel like enough. He's looking for her to complete something he hasn't let God complete first.

  • The problem isn't the desire. It's the order.
  • Formation first. Addition second. That's the pattern.

Longing is the door. Becoming is the work before you walk through it.
