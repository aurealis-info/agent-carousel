  1. 1. Is my private life something I'd be ashamed for God to see?

    It already is. That's either a comfort or a warning.

  2. 2. What am I feeding more than I'm feeding my faith?

    Whatever gets the most of your attention is growing. Something is winning.

  3. 3. Who has real permission to call me out — and will I actually listen?

    Accountability without honesty is just a meeting.

  4. 4. Am I pursuing God, or just the man I want to become?

    Formation is God's work in you. Self-improvement is your work on yourself. The order matters.

  5. 5. What sin am I still managing instead of killing?

    Scripture doesn't say manage it. It says put it to death.
