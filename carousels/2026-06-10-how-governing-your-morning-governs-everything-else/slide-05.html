Step 4

Carry what you built into the rest of the day

The point of the morning isn't the morning. It's the man who walks out of it.

What you built before 8 AM doesn't disappear at 9. It runs underneath everything.

  • Your morning is rehearsal for who shows up at noon, at 7 PM, at midnight.
  • One good morning, then another. That's how the man gets built.

The man who governs his morning governs everything that follows.
