Step 1

Check where the scrutiny goes

Most guys have it flipped. They'll explain away their own sin for a week and pick apart their girlfriend's tone in one sentence.

Notice which direction your judgment travels by default. If it's always outward, that's the tell.

  • Ask before you speak: am I this hard on my own sin?
  • If the answer's no, hold the comment.

The scrutiny that never turns inward isn't conviction. It's cover.
