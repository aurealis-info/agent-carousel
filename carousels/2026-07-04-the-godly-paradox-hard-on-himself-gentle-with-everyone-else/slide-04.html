Step 3

Save the anger for your own sin

Jesus wept over a city. He also flipped tables in a temple. Both were real. The difference is where each one landed.

He never turned that fire on the people who came to him broken. He saved it for what was rotting the house of God. Aim yours the same way.

  • Get angrier at your own compromise than at their mistake.
  • Bring the fire to your sin. Bring the gentleness to theirs.

He never wept over the broken and raged at the mess he made himself.
