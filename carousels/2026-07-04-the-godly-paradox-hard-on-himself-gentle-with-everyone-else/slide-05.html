Step 4

Hand out grace before you're asked

The order matters. Grace to others isn't the reward for having your own life together. It's the overflow of knowing how much grace you needed first.

A man who remembers what he was forgiven for doesn't make people earn his patience.

  • Extend it before he apologizes, not after.
  • Remember your own worst week before you judge someone else's.

He who's forgiven much finds it easy to forgive.
