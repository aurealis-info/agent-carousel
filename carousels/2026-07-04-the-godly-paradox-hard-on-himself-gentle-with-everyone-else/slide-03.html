Step 2

Give away the patience you keep for yourself

You already know how to be patient with someone who's slipping. You do it for yourself constantly. "I'm working on it. I'll get there."

Hand that same patience to the people around you before you hand yourself another excuse.

  • Next time she's slow to get it, give her the grace you gave yourself yesterday.
  • Keep the deadline you set for yourself, not just the one you set for others.

Whatever grace you're rationing, spend it on him or her first.
