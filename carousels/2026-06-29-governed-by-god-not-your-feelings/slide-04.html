Step 3

Do what you said you'd do

Discipline is proof. The man who shows up when it costs him something — who keeps his word to his brother, to God, and to himself even when the feeling wants to quit — that man is becoming trustworthy.

That's not suppression. That's freedom.

  • Keep the commitment. Drop the feeling.
  • The feeling will catch up — or it won't. Keep moving either way.
