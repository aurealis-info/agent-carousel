Step 1

Notice before you move

Every feeling is information.

Before you text back, speak up, walk away, or go quiet — pause. Name what's happening inside. Anger. Fear. Shame. The need to be seen. Name it out loud if you have to.

  • One question buys you back the wheel: "What is this actually about?"

Notice it before it decides for you.
