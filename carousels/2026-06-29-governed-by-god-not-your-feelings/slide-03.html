Step 2

Ask who's running the show

Once you've named the feeling, ask the honest question: is Christ steering this, or is the emotion?

Not because feelings are wrong. Because a man under God's authority makes choices — not reactions.

  • A man ruled by his moods is a city with no walls.
  • A man governed by Christ can feel everything and still choose.

"Where the Spirit of the Lord is, there is freedom." — 2 Cor. 3:17
