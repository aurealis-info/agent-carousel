Step 2

Romans 8:13 doesn't say manage it

Paul's word is "put to death." That is not the language of coping strategies. It's not "reduce" or "find balance" or "be patient with yourself."

"If by the Spirit you put to death the deeds of the body, you will live."

The killing is the point. If you're still managing it, you haven't started yet.

God is not asking you to improve the old man. He's asking you to bury him.
