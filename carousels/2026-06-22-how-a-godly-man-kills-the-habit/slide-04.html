Step 3

Cut the supply line, not just the craving

Willpower is not the weapon. The habit doesn't die from trying harder. It dies when you remove what feeds it.

  • What's the trigger? Late night, certain apps, certain rooms?
  • Remove access before the craving shows up, not after.

The man who fights on the same ground every time will lose eventually. Change the ground.

Remove the fuel. The fire goes out.
