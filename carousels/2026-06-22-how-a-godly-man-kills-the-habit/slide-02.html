Step 1

Name it, out loud

You cannot fight a thing you won't name. Vague is safe. Vague is also where things don't die.

Laziness. The scroll. The private tab. The bitterness you've worn for two years. Give it a word.

  • Say it to one person — not a journal, a person.
  • You're not confessing to shame yourself. You're removing the secrecy that feeds it.

The unnamed thing has all the power. The named thing starts to lose it.
