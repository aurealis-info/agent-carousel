Step 4

Your prayer life when nothing's forcing it

When there's no crisis, no deadline, no exam the next morning: do you still go to God? Or only when you need something?

The man whose faith only appears under pressure doesn't have a relationship. He has a rescue plan.

  • Open your Bible on the Tuesday when nothing's wrong.
  • Talk to Him when you have nothing to ask for. That's the real conversation.

The man who seeks God when he doesn't have to is the man who finds Him when he needs to.
