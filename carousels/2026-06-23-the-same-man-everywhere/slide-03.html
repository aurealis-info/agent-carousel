How you treat people when you're tired

Patience when it's easy is not a virtue. Patience when you're exhausted and nobody's watching. That's character.

The man who is kind at church and cold at home has built a performance, not a life. The people closest to you get the real man. Make sure they'd recognize him from your feed.

Christlikeness is for the Tuesday, not just the Sunday.
