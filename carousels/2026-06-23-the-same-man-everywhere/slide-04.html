Step 3

What you do with the idle hour

The unplanned hour is the most honest one. There's no accountability, no audience, no reason to look good. What you reach for is what you actually love.

A man whose idle time looks nothing like his values hasn't changed. He's just managed his appearance.

  • Let the idle hour be one you'd be willing to account for.
  • Not because someone's watching. Because you're becoming someone.

Character is what you do when there's nothing to prove.
