ETHOS

  • 6. Tells the truth even when the half-truth would have worked.

    Integrity isn't a performance. It's a private habit.

  • 7. Takes care of what's already his.

    The home. The body. The commitments. A man who handles what he has can be trusted with more.

  • 8. Sits with what's hard instead of scrolling past it.

    The discomfort is data. The man who can be with it grows. The one who runs from it stays stuck.

  • 9. Gets back up the same day he fell.

    He doesn't call the week done. He doesn't wait for Monday. He gets up.

  • 10. Ends the day with thanks, not the feed.

    Gratitude is a practice, not a feeling. Do it tired.
