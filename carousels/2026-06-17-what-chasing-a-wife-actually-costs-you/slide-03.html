Step 2

The man you are single is the man she's choosing

You don't become a different man once she says yes. The discipline, the faithfulness, the way you treat people when no one's watching — that's what you're bringing her.

Your character before marriage is your character in it. She's not choosing the future version you're planning to become. She's choosing the man who's here right now.

  • Build now. Not to impress her. Because it's right.
  • The man formed in private is the one she'll live with.
