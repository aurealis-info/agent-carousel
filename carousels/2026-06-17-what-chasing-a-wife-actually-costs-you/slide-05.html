Step 4

A man who needs her to complete him will drain her

Marriage doesn't fix the emptiness. It exposes it.

You're called to lead. You can't lead well from a place of need. Walk with God until the emptiness is the right kind — the kind only He fills. Then bring her a man who's whole, not a man looking for rescue.

She deserves a man who chose her from fullness, not one who found her from hunger.

Become the man first. That's what Scripture asks.
