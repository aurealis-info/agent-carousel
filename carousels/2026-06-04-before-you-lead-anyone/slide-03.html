Trait 2

Tell the truth to the right person

Good leaders say the hard thing face to face.

Most men talk around the conflict — in group chats, to anyone but the guy who needs to hear it.

"Better is open rebuke than hidden love." — Proverbs 27:5

  • When you need to say something, say it to him — not about him.

The man who won't confront can't lead. He can only manage.
