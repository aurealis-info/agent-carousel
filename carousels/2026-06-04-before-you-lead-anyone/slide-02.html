Trait 1

Serve where no one's watching

The man God promotes is the one nobody had to ask.

He took out the trash. He followed through on the small thing. He wasn't waiting for a title to start doing the work.

  • Find the job no one volunteers for.
  • Do it well. Don't mention it.

Faithfulness in small things is the only résumé God reads.
