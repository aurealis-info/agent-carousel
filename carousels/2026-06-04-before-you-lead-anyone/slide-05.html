Get under authority

The centurion understood power because he was under power.

Jesus marveled at that man — Matthew 8. A soldier who knew how to follow.

Who you submit to right now is forming who you'll lead tomorrow.

  • Name the men you're under. Are you actually teachable to them?

You can't lead well what you haven't learned to follow.
