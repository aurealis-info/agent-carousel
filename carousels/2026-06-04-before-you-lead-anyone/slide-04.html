Go first into the cost

Jesus didn't ask his disciples to die for him. He died first.

That's the pattern. First to apologize. First to forgive. First to take the hit.

Leadership isn't "I'm in charge — follow me." It's "I paid — now watch."

  • Next time the cost is real, ask who should go first.
  • Then go.

The man worth following already paid.
