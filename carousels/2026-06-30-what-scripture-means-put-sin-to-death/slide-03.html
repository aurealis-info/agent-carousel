Step 2

You don't kill it by trying harder

Trying harder in the same environment changes nothing. You kill a sin by cutting off what feeds it.

Delete the app. Cancel the subscription. Change the route. Restructure the conditions, not just the resolve.

  • Name what feeds it.
  • Then build a wall between you and that exact thing.

The man who changes his conditions changes the outcome.
