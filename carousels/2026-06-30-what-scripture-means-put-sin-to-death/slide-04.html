Step 3

Name the exact thing, then starve it

"I struggle with sin" kills nothing. Name the specific thing that owns you at 11 PM. Name the exact trigger, the exact moment it takes over.

Then make a plan to starve that exact thing. Vague awareness keeps sin comfortable. Precision is the scalpel.

  • Write it down. The moment you can name it, you can address it.
  • One wall, one sin, one concrete change this week.

Vague conviction changes nothing. Name it.
