Step 4

Confession is where the killing starts

What you hide stays alive. The sin that never saw the light of another man's face has never actually been surrendered.

"Confess your sins to one another, and pray for each other, that you may be healed." — James 5:16

One honest brother. One real conversation. Light is the weapon sin can't survive.

  • Find one man who can hold this without flinching.
  • Say the exact thing out loud. That's where it starts to die.
