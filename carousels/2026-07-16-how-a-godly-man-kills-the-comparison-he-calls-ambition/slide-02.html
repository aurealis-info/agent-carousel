Step 1

Name it when it shows up as a goal

Envy rarely announces itself. It shows up disguised as a goal you suddenly need.

The raise you didn't want last year. The platform. The body. The relationship. It got urgent the day someone else got it.

  • Ask why this goal got loud right now.
  • If the answer is someone else's life, it's envy, not calling.
