Step 3

Put it to death, don't negotiate with it

Scripture doesn't say manage your envy. Colossians puts it right next to greed and malice: put it to death.

You don't talk envy down. You stop feeding it and let it starve.

  • Mute the feed that feeds it.
  • Say the ugly thing out loud to a brother. It loses power the moment it's named.
