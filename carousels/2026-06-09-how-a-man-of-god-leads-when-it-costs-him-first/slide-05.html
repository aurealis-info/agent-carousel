Step 4

Lead from the floor, not the throne.

The man everyone follows is usually the one they've seen get up off the floor. Not the one who never fell — the one who didn't stay down, and didn't pretend he hadn't been down.

"Whoever wants to be first must be slave of all." — Mark 10:44. That's not a metaphor. It's a job description.

  • Be the first to serve, not just the first to decide.
  • Your position is a responsibility, not a reward.

The man worth following goes into the cost first.
