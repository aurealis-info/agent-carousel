Step 3

Stay when it stops being impressive.

The hardest part of leadership isn't starting. It's being present on the days when no one's watching and nothing's working.

Quitting is easy — especially when you can frame it as wisdom. But staying when it gets quiet and grinding when the crowd's gone is where the man actually forms.

  • Show up on the ugly Tuesdays, not just the launch days.
  • The people you lead will know the difference.

Faithfulness is just endurance with a better name.
