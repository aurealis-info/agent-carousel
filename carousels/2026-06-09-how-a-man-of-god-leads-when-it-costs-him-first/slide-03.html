Step 2

Absorb before you assign.

Before you push a problem down to someone else, ask what you could absorb up. A weak leader moves everything difficult off his plate. A strong one asks, "What part of this is mine to carry?"

Christ didn't delegate the cross. He walked into the cost on behalf of the people he was responsible for.

  • Take the hard call yourself before you hand it off.
  • Carry what they can't before you ask them to carry more.

Absorbing is the move. Assigning is the shortcut.
