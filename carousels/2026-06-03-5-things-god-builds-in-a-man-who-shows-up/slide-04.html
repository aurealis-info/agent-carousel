Thing 3

Who you actually are

You don’t find yourself. You build yourself — and the hard years show you what you made.

Where you fold: that’s where the work still is.

Where you hold: that’s what God already built.

  • Pay attention to where you break under pressure.
  • That’s not failure. That’s a map.

Hard years don’t just form you. They reveal you.
