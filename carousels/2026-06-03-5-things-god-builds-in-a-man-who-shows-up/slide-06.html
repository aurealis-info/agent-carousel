Thing 5

Built to carry weight

A wife. A family. A team. A calling. These things need a man underneath them who’s been built.

“Act like men. Be strong.” — 1 Cor 16:13. Paul wasn’t writing to easy men.

Your 20s are where that foundation goes in. Coasting now means borrowing against a foundation you haven’t laid.

  • What are you building today that will hold weight in ten years?
  • That’s the only question.

You’re not just becoming someone. You’re becoming someone capable.
