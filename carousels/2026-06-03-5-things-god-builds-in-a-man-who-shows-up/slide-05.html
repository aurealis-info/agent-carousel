Thing 4

A name worth something

In your 20s, almost no one knows your name. Good.

You’re building a track record, not a platform.

The men who’ll trust you with the important things later are watching the small things now.

  • Be trustworthy when there’s nothing in it for you.
  • That’s what a reputation actually is.

Reputation is just character confirmed over time.
