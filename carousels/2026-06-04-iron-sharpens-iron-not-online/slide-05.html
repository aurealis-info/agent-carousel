Step 4

One man. Real answers.

You don't need a group. You need one man.

Someone who asks three things and expects the truth:

Are you in the Word? Are you clean? What aren't you telling me?

  • Pick someone you respect enough to be fully honest with.
  • Name the standard before you start — what are we accountable to?

One man who tells you the truth is worth a thousand who clap.
