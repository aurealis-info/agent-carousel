Step 2

Confess, not post

James 5:16 says confess your sins to each other.

Not your prayer request. Your sin. The specific one.

The man who knows it picks up at midnight and asks by name.

  • He knows what you did — not that you had a rough week.
  • That conversation happened in person, not in a thread.

You can't be held accountable by a server.
