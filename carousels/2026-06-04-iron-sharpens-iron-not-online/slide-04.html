Contact, not content

Online, you control what they see.

The verse you posted. The PR you hit. The win.

A real brother sees the Tuesday at 3 AM version — the one you'd never post.

  • Physical presence changes what you'll admit out loud.
  • The thing you'd text but not say is the thing that needs to be said.

Iron sharpens iron with contact, not content.
