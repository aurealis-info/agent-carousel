Step 4

Carry one line into your day

Most men forget what they read within the hour. You don't need to retain a chapter — just one phrase. Let it ride with you.

At your desk. In the gym. On the commute. "Do your work heartily, as to the Lord." — Colossians 3:23. That line changes the meeting.

  • One remembered line is better than a forgotten chapter.
  • The Word does work in you when you keep it in front of you.

Scripture carried into the day is Scripture doing its job.
