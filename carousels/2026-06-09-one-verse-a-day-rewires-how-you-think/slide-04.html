Step 3

Let it land before you run

After you read it — sit for 30 seconds. Ask: where does this hit me today?

The man who reads Scripture and immediately opens Instagram gave the ground back. Let the Word take root before the noise takes over.

  • Write one line if something hits — the act of writing locks it in.
  • Don't reach for the phone until the Word has had a minute.

"Let the word of Christ dwell in you richly." — Colossians 3:16.
