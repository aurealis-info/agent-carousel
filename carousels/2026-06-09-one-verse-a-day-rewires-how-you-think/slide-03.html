Step 2

One verse, slowly

Not a chapter. Not a reading plan you'll feel guilty about when you fall behind. One verse. Read it twice.

Ask what it says about God. Then ask what it says about you. That's the whole practice.

  • 60 seconds of encounter beats a chapter skimmed at speed.
  • The Word is alive. Engage with it like it actually is.

You're not reading for information. You're reading to be changed.
