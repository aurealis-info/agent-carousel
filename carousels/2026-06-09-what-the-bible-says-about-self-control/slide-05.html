Step 4

The trust that follows

A man who governs himself gets trusted with more — the relationship, the responsibility, the room.

You can't lead what you can't rule first. The governed man is the one people follow, not because he demands it, but because they know he's already won the harder battle inside.

  • Start with the small thing. Govern that. Then the next.
  • The compound interest of small obedience is a man nobody saw coming.

You govern yourself — or something else does it for you.
