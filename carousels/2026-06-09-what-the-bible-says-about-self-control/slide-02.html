Step 1

A city without walls

Proverbs 25:28 — "A man without self-control is like a city broken into and left without walls."

That's not a moral lecture. That's a vulnerability assessment. The enemy doesn't need to fight a governed man — he walks through an open gate.

  • The open gate is the alarm you don't obey.
  • The open gate is the door you keep reopening at night.

The ungoverned man isn't free. He's exposed.
