Step 2

It's a fruit, not a muscle

Galatians 5:23 — self-control is a fruit of the Spirit. That's not a metaphor for "try harder."

You don't build it by white-knuckling. You grow it by staying close to the vine. The man who grits his way to discipline without God isn't governed — he's just temporarily contained.

  • Striving without the Spirit produces performance, not transformation.
  • Stay in the Word. The fruit grows where the roots go.

You can't out-discipline your way to what only the Spirit grows.
