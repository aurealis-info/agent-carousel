Step 3

Your three gates

The phone in your hand. The alarm you don't obey. The door you keep opening in the dark.

You don't have to govern your whole life at once. Govern those three and everything downstream shifts.

  • The morning is where the day is decided — own the first hour.
  • The phone is where lust, envy, and wasted time live. Treat it like a gate.
  • The thing you keep reopening at night — close it tonight. Not tomorrow.
