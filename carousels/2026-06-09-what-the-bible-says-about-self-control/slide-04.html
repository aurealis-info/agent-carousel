Step 3

It grows — you don't force it

"The fruit of the Spirit is… self-control." — Galatians 5:23

The man growing in self-control is the man walking in the Spirit.

You don't produce it by trying harder. You stay near the Source and it grows.

  • The discipline follows the devotion.

You're not the manufacturer. You're the soil.
