Seek first — and trust the order

"Seek first the kingdom of God and his righteousness, and all these things will be added to you." — Matthew 6:33.

The man who makes that his actual operating system — not just his Sunday answer — becomes whole without her. Which is exactly what makes him ready to pursue her rightly when the time comes.

  • Be the kind of man God can trust to lead a woman well.

The man who needs nothing from her can give everything to her.
