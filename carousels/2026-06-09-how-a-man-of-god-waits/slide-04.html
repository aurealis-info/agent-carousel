Step 3

The wait is the work

Most men in the wait are drifting or striving. Both are running from it.

But the wilderness is where God does some of His best formation. Joseph didn't become the right man for the moment in the palace — he became him in the pit and the prison.

The wait is not a gap in your story. It is the story.

  • Let Him finish the work. Don't rush the season.
