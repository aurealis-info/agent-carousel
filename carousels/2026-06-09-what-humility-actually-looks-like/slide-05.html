Step 4

It kills the need to win every room

The humble man doesn't shrink from conflict — he just doesn't need to be right about everything. Correction can land. Credit can go elsewhere. Silence can work where words would only flex.

The proud man burns energy defending himself. The humble man uses it to build.

"God opposes the proud but gives grace to the humble." — James 4:6
