Step 4

Replace the habit, not just break it

A man who removes the scroll without filling the space is back within a week.

"Set your mind on things above." — Colossians 3:2. That's not a mood. It's a practice. Don't just vacate the throne — replace the king on it.

  • What is your first act of worship tomorrow morning?
  • Set it up tonight, before you go to sleep.

Replace the throne. Don't just leave it empty.
