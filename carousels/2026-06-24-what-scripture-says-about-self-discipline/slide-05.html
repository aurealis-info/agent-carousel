The goal is freedom, not a performance review.

You don't discipline yourself to become someone God can use. You discipline yourself because Christ already has hold of you. The difference is everything. One is striving for approval. The other is growing into it. "It is for freedom that Christ has set us free." — Galatians 5:1. Discipline rooted in that is not a burden. It's what a free man does.
