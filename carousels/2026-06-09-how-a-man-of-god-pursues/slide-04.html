Step 3

Lead With Who You're Becoming

A man of God doesn't sell himself. He shows up doing the work — in his faith, in his friendships, in the hard things he's been running from.

What you're becoming matters more than what you're offering.

  • The work you do on yourself is the attraction.
  • Let your life speak before your words do.

She's looking for a man going somewhere. Be the man going somewhere.
