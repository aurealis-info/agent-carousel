Step 1

Know What You're Actually Building

The guy searching desperately for a wife is building his identity on her.

He'll bring his emptiness into the relationship — not his character.

She doesn't need a man who needs her to be okay. She needs a man who's already building something.

  • Work on the man in the mirror before you go looking.
  • Let her find a man who's already whole.

You can't give her what you haven't built yet.
