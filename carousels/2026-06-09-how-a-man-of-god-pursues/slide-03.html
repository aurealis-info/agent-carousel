Step 2

Pursue Openly, Not Desperately

Desperation isn't attractive — it's a warning signal.

Open pursuit is clear, direct, and holds its own ground. You ask plainly. You don't orbit and hint and wait for her to make it easy.

  • Ask clearly. State your interest. Let her answer.
  • If she says no, respect it and release it — that's what a man does.

The desperate man loses her before he starts.
