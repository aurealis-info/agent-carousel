Step 4

Let It Reveal You, Not Define You

A relationship shows you who you are under pressure.

If she becomes the thing your identity hangs on, it'll break under the weight — and so will she.

"Delight yourself in the Lord, and he will give you the desires of your heart." — Psalm 37:4.

  • Chase God first. Let everything else follow.
  • The man who needs her too much can't actually lead her.

Get the order right, and you become the man she was praying for.
