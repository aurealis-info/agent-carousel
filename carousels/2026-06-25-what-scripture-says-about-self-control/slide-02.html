Willpower runs out. The Spirit doesn't.

Galatians 5:22-23 lists self-control as a fruit of the Spirit — not an output of discipline. That's a different category. A fruit grows from a root. You don't manufacture it. You tend the root and let it come. Every study on willpower says the same thing: it depletes. What God gave you for this does not work the same way.
