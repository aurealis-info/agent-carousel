He takes the hard seat first

First to apologize. First to serve. First in on the thing nobody wants.

Not because he was told to — because that's the man he decided to be.

Jesus washed feet. He didn't wait to be asked.

  • "The greatest among you shall be your servant." — Matthew 23:11.
