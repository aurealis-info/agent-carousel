Trait 5

He gives God the credit and means it

Not as a caption formality.

He actually knows the gifts were placed in him — they weren't earned.

So the success doesn't puff him up. The failure doesn't undo him.

He's grounded in something the room doesn't control.

  • "Act justly. Love mercy. Walk humbly with your God." — Micah 6:8.

A man built on that won't blow down.
