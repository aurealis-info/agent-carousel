Trait 4

He can take a correction

Most men fold or fight when they're told they're wrong.

The man who walks humbly hears it and holds it.

He asks: is there something true here?

He doesn't need to protect a reputation God hasn't confirmed anyway.

  • Don't respond immediately. Sit with it first.

Teachable isn't weak. It's the fastest way to grow.
