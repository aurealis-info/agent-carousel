Step 2

Find the root, not the habit

The behavior is a symptom. The porn, the lying, the rage — each one is running from something underneath: loneliness, fear, the need to control.

A man who only fights the behavior is in a war he can't win. Cut the root and the branch dies with it.

  • Ask what you're trying to feel, escape, or prove.
  • That's where the real fight is.
