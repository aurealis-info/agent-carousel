Step 1

Confess it by name

Not "I've been struggling." Name the actual thing. Vague confession is no confession.

Name it the way a surgeon names what he's cutting out. Say it to one man who knows you.

  • James 5:16 is not a suggestion. It's the mechanism: confess, and healing follows.
  • What stays hidden in the dark stays alive.
