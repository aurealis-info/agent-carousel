Step 4

Replace the void

Colossians 3:5 says "put to death." Colossians 3:12 says "put on." They go together.

Killing something leaves a gap. A man who doesn't fill it with something from God will fill it with the sin on its way back in. The Word. The brother. The mission. Something alive.

  • Mortification without replacement is just willpower with better branding.
