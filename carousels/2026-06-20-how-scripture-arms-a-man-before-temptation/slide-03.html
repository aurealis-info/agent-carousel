Step 2

Name the pattern before it names you

Temptation doesn't appear out of nowhere. There is a time it hits. A mood it follows. A door you keep walking past.

The man who knows his pattern can work with God to close the door before he opens it.

  • When does it show up? Be specific about the hour and the mood.
  • What preceded it the last three times? That's the real entry point.

The man who can name his weakness is harder to ambush.
