Step 4

Cut the route, not just the behavior

You don't beat a temptation by being more disciplined at the moment it hits. You beat it by removing the access two steps earlier.

Delete the app. Move the device out of the room. Put friction between yourself and the sin before you're weak enough not to care.

  • What access does this sin need to reach you?
  • Remove one route this week. One is enough to start.

Discipline at the moment of temptation is too late. Do the work before you get there.
