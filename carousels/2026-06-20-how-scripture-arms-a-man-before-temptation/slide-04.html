Step 3

Confess it to one man before it owns you

James 5:16: "Confess your sins to one another, that you may be healed."

Not to a stage. Not a small group icebreaker. One man, eye to eye, who's allowed to ask the question you're hoping he doesn't.

  • Text one man this week before you feel like you need to.
  • Let him ask the hard question. Give him the real answer.

Light is the only thing that breaks what darkness has been building.
