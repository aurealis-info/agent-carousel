Step 1

Put the Word in before the battle starts

You don't load the weapon when you're already being shot at.

The time to know what God says about lust, anger, or cowardice is before the situation. Not during it. Five verses at 6 AM changes what surfaces at noon.

  • Open Scripture before you open your phone.
  • One line that stays with you is better than a chapter you scroll through.

The man who's already in the Word is harder to catch off guard.
