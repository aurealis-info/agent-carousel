Step 3

What to put in the silence

Most men fill the morning with noise before the noise of the day starts. Podcast on. Music on. Phone in hand.

But the silence of 5 AM isn't empty — it's where God gets a word in. One chapter, one prayer, a few minutes of just sitting still.

You'll be surprised how loud He is when you're finally quiet.

  • Sit still long enough to hear something that isn't you.
