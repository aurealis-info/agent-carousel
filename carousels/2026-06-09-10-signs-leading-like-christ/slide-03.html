  1. 6. You protect people who can't protect themselves.

    The weak, the overlooked, the one who won't fight back. That's the room Christ walked into.

  2. 7. You can be corrected without it costing the relationship.

    Pride ends correction. Humility ends distance. Christ submitted to the Father — so can you.

  3. 8. You lead the same in private as in public.

    The gap between those two is the gap between your leadership and his character.

  4. 9. You don't need credit for the things that go right.

    The secure leader makes others look capable. The insecure one can't afford to.

  5. 10. The people you lead trust you — and they can say why.

    Trust is the only currency that counts. Build it slowly. Spend it carefully.
