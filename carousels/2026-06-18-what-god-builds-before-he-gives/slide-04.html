Step 3

He tends what he's already been given

You don't get more by ignoring what you have. The man who's faithful with little doesn't get handed more because he's been patient — he gets more because he's already proven he can carry it.

"Whoever can be trusted with very little can also be trusted with much." — Luke 16:10

  • Take care of your body, your word, your room.
  • Faithfulness in the small things is how the big things start.

The man who takes care of what he has is already being prepared for what's coming.
