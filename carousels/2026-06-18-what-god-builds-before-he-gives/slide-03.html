Step 2

He does hard work without applause

The men who've built anything worth keeping all say the same thing: the years nobody saw were the ones that made them.

Hard work without applause isn't punishment. It's formation. The character you're building in the quiet is what you'll need when things actually get hard.

  • Don't need someone to clap to know you did the right thing.
  • The quiet work is where character is made, not just revealed.

God sees the rep nobody else counted.
