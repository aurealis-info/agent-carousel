Step 1

He shows up before anyone's watching

Most men want the platform before the preparation. But what God builds in the hidden season is what holds the weight later.

The unglamorous faithfulness — the early morning, the quiet obedience, the kept word when no one checked — that's not nothing. That's everything.

  • Open your Bible before your schedule.
  • Do the thing you said you'd do, even when no one asked for an update.

The faithfulness God rewards most isn't the kind anyone photographed.
