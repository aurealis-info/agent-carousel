Step 4

He learns to wait without going sideways

Waiting is where men either get formed or fall apart. The idle season becomes the reckless season — or it becomes the deepest season of his life.

The difference is what he does with it. Stay in the Word. Stay in the room. Stay faithful. The waiting isn't wasted time. It's the work.

  • Don't sprint ahead of what God's already doing.
  • Faithfulness in the waiting season is still faithfulness.

The man who learns to wait is already being given something.
