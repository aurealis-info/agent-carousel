Step 1

Stop hedging your yes

You say "I'll try." "Probably." "We'll see." Every hedge is a door left open so you can bail without looking like you bailed.

A godly man's yes doesn't need an escape hatch built into it.

  • Say the plain word. Yes or no.
  • Drop the qualifier that's just there to protect your exit.
