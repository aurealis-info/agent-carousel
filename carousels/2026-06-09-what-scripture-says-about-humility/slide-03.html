02

The humble man can't be controlled.

The man who doesn't need approval can't be manipulated by it. The man who doesn't need credit can give everything away. The man who doesn't need the last word can let it go.

"God opposes the proud but gives grace to the humble." — James 4:6.

Worldly power is built on proving yourself. Biblical humility is built on not needing to. That's not weakness. That's freedom.
