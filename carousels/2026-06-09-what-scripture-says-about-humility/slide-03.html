Step 2

The humble man can look anyone in the eye.

He's not crumbling, apologizing for existing, or playing small. He's sturdy. He doesn't need to inflate because he's not afraid of deflating.

"Do justice. Love kindness. Walk humbly with your God." — Micah 6:8. Justice and kindness come before it. The humble man is doing things, not just being small.

  • Humility is a posture, not a posture of defeat.
  • Sturdy and lowly are not opposites.

The man who doesn't need to be big is often the biggest man in the room.
