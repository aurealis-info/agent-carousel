Step 1

It's not thinking less of yourself.

C.S. Lewis said the humble man doesn't think less of himself — he thinks of himself less. That's not weakness. That's someone who's got the source right.

The arrogant man is obsessed with his own standing. The humble man doesn't need to be, because he knows whose he is.

  • Humility is accurate. Arrogance is delusion.
  • Know who made you — that's where it starts.

You can only think clearly about yourself when you're clear about God.
