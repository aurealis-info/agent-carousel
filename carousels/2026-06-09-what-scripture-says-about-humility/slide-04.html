Step 3

Humility is what lets you lead.

Moses led a nation. Numbers 12 calls him the most humble man on earth. Those two facts aren't in tension — they're the same fact twice.

Pride locks you into the version of the story where you're always right. Humility keeps you moveable. A man who can be corrected can be trusted with more.

  • The unteachable man stops growing the day he decides he's done learning.
  • The humble man absorbs correction and gets stronger.

Pride makes you brittle. Humility makes you durable.
