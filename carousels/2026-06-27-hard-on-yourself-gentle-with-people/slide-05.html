Step 4

Jesus wept. He also flipped tables.

Both were right. Both were necessary. And he never mixed them up.

He wept over the city. He drove out the men profiting from the temple. Tenderness for people in pain. Fierceness for sin that harms.

A man of faith learns the difference. Fierce about his own sin. Tender toward people who fail. Not the other way around.

Fierceness for righteousness. Tenderness for people. Get them in the right places.
