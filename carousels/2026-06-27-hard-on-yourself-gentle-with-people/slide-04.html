Step 3

Point your energy inward

Every unit of energy you spend scrutinizing someone else's obedience is energy borrowed from your own formation.

You don't have enough to run both. You have to pick.

The man whose hands are full with his own sanctification rarely has time to audit anyone else's. That's not an accident.

  • Drop the commentary. Pick up the work.
  • Your formation is a full-time job.

The best thing you can do for the people around you is to become who you're being formed to be.
