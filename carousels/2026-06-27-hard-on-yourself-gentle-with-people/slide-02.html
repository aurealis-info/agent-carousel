Step 1

Know which one you're doing

Hard on others shows up in a hundred quiet ways. The sharp word. The eye-roll at someone else's failure. The grace you'd give a stranger but not your own family.

Soft on yourself shows up just as quietly. The sin you call a weakness. The conviction that passes without landing. The accountability you agreed to but never actually gave.

Most men don't know they've flipped it. The first step is honest enough to look.

You can't fix what you won't name.
