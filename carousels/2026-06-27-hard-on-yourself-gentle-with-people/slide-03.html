Step 2

Hold yourself to what you'd hold a brother to

If you'd sit across from a friend and call out his laziness, call it out in the mirror first.

If you'd tell a younger man his anger is a problem, your anger is the same problem.

The standard doesn't lower when it's you. It might need to be higher, because you have more clarity about your own sin than anyone else does.

Accountability you won't give yourself isn't accountability. It's theater.
