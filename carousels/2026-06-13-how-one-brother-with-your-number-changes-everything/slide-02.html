Know the difference: follower vs. brother

A podcast host can have 50,000 followers and not one man to call at 2 a.m.

Brotherhood isn't subscriptions. It's two men who actually have each other's number.

  • Not a Discord server.
  • Not a small group icebreaker.

The man who can call you is the man who can hold you.
