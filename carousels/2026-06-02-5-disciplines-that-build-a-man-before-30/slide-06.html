Discipline 5

Govern the screen

Your phone is where lust lives, where envy lives, where your time dies.

If you can't put it down, something other than Christ owns that part of you.

  • Set the phone on the other side of the room before you sleep.
  • Keep it there in the morning — until you've given the first hour to God.

A man who governs his phone can govern himself.
