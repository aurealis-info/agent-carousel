Discipline 3

Build in private

Your character is what you do when there's no one watching.

"Work at it with all your heart, as working for the Lord." — Colossians 3:23

  • The rep God saw at 5 AM is the one that counts.
  • Consistency with no audience is the highest form of the work.

Hidden faithfulness is the foundation of public character.
