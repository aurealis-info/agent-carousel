What Paul Actually Said

A fruit, not a technique

Paul names self-control in Galatians 5. But not as a skill to master.

He calls it a fruit — of the Spirit.

Fruit doesn't come from flexing. It grows from roots.

  • A man grinding through willpower is fighting something.
  • A man rooted in the Spirit starts to want different things.

You don't produce fruit. You stay connected to the vine.
