What it actually runs on

Paul trains like an athlete. 1 Corinthians 9:27: "I discipline my body and keep it under control."

The discipline is real. The training is real.

But the goal isn't performance. It's accountability to something bigger than the moment.

  • A man under authority governs himself.
  • A man under pressure just grits his teeth.
  • Teeth unclench eventually.

Authority outlasts willpower.
