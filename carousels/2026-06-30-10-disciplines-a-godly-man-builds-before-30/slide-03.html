  1. 6. A budget, run on paper every week.

    A man who can't govern his money can't govern himself. And the money always knows before he does.

  2. 7. Ten minutes of silence. No device.

    You can't hear God over your own noise. The man who can't sit in silence doesn't know himself yet.

  3. 8. A skill or trade he can point to.

    Be useful. Not impressive — useful. The 20s are for getting built.

  4. 9. A real apology, made in person.

    No caveats. No "if you were hurt." Just: I was wrong. Those three words are a discipline very few men have mastered.

  5. 10. One brother who knows his actual sin.

    Not the polished version. The real one. That relationship is worth building now.
