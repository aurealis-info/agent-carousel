Know what you're for

A man who doesn't know where he's going will take her nowhere.

Not a résumé. A direction — work he's called to, something he's building, a reason to get up that isn't her.

  • She's not your purpose. She's someone you carry your purpose alongside.

The man with no mission makes her the mission. She can't hold that weight.
