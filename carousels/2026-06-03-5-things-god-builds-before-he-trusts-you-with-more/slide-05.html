Thing 4

The humility to keep being shaped

The man who thinks he’s arrived has stopped being formed.

A man God uses in his 40s is still being taught in his 20s — still willing to hear hard things, still choosing the mentor over the audience.

  • Ask the question that makes you look like you don’t have it figured out.
  • That’s the one God’s been waiting for.

“Do you see a man wise in his own eyes? There is more hope for a fool than for him.” — Proverbs 26:12
