Thing 5

Contentment without complacency

Restlessness isn’t ambition. It’s evidence that something besides God has hold of you.

You can want more and still be at peace in the thing he put you in right now. That tension is the test.

The man God trusts with more is the man who didn’t let the waiting make him bitter.

Content and hungry at once — that’s the man ready for the next thing.
