Thing 1

Faithfulness when no one’s watching

You want the bigger stage. God wants to see if you’ll show up to the smaller one when no one’s watching.

The man trusted with much is always the one who was faithful first with little — and kept being faithful when nothing came from it.

  • Show up to your current job the way you’d want your future team to show up.
  • The reps no one sees are the ones building the man.

What you do when it costs you nothing is who you’ll be when it costs you everything.
