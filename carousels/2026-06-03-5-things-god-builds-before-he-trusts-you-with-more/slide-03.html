Thing 2

Prayer that outlasts the silence

You want the breakthrough. God wants to know if you still pray when the breakthrough doesn’t come.

Not the performance of it. The actual practice — on your knees when nothing’s happening, when it feels like you’re talking to yourself.

  • Keep the morning even when the morning feels empty.
  • Silence isn’t abandonment. It’s formation.

The prayer life that survives the silence is the one worth having.
