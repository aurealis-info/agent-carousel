The patience to wait

Most men don't disqualify themselves because they're bad — they disqualify themselves because they can't wait.

They push. They grab for it early and wonder why it doesn't hold. Joseph sat in a prison he didn't deserve. The timing was God's, not his.

  • The man who forces the door often breaks what was supposed to come through it.

If you can't hold tension, you can't be trusted with weight.
