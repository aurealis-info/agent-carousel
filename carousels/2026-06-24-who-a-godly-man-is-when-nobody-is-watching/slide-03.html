Step 2

How you treat people who can't help you

The waiter. The cashier. Your mom when you're tired and running late.

The person with no platform, no leverage, nothing to offer you. That's the test. Not the handshake with the pastor. Not the way you act when the right person is watching.

  • Kindness that costs you nothing isn't character.
  • Kindness when you're drained and nobody's looking — that is.

The version of you that shows up when there's nothing to gain — that's the man you actually are.
