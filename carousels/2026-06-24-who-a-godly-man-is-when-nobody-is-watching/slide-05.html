Step 4

The habit you keep managing instead of killing

You know what it is. The pattern that runs you after the lights go out.

Integrity isn't having no sin. It's not having two selves. One version for the church, one version for the midnight hour. A man who's the same in both rooms is a man being formed — not performing.

  • Name it. Bring one man into it. Stop feeding it.
  • The gap between public and private is where a man drifts.

What you hide grows. What you confess starts to die.
