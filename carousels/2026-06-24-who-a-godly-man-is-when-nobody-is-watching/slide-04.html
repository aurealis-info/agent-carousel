Step 3

What your prayer life looks like alone

Not the verse you posted. Not the way you pray in a group when it's your turn.

The room with a closed door on a Tuesday when nobody's watching and nothing's rewarded. That's where the actual conversation with God lives.

"When you pray, go into your room, close the door and pray to your Father, who is unseen." — Matthew 6:6

The prayer no one hears is the one that actually forms you.
