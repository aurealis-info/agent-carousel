  1. 1. Real men don't need anyone.

    Isolation isn't strength. It's where men rot. Even Paul needed Timothy.

  2. 2. Showing emotion means you've lost control.

    Jesus wept at Lazarus's grave. He also cleared the temple. Both were strength. Neither cancelled the other.

  3. 3. Lead by being the loudest in the room.

    The calmest man is usually the one everyone actually follows.

  4. 4. If your faith is strong enough, you won't be tempted.

    Paul said "I do the very thing I hate." Christ was tempted in the wilderness. Faith doesn't remove the test.
