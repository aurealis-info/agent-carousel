Step 1

Pick one appetite this week

Not to punish it. To prove it doesn't run you. Skip the dessert you'd normally have. Skip the show you'd normally binge. Small, specific, on purpose.

  • Pick something you'd actually miss, not something easy to skip.
  • Tell no one. This isn't a performance.

The point isn't the thing you gave up. It's who was in charge when you did.
