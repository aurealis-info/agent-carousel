Step 2

Build it before you need it

1 Corinthians 9:27 says Paul disciplined his body and kept it under control, so that after preaching to others he himself wouldn't be disqualified.

He wasn't waiting for a crisis to practice self-control. He built it on purpose, in small moments no one was grading.

  • Self-control you've never practiced doesn't show up the day you need it most.

The discipline is the point. The dessert was never the point.
