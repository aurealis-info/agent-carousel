Step 3

Let the small no prove the big one

The muscle that skips dessert on a Tuesday is the same muscle that walks away from the scroll at 1 a.m., the same one that says no to the money he shouldn't spend.

You don't build it in the emergency. You build it in the boring rep nobody sees.

  • The voluntary no is practice for the involuntary one.
  • A man who's never told himself no doesn't know if he can.

Train the no you don't need yet. You'll need it eventually.
