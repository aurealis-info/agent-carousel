Trait 5

Lead yourself first

He makes his bed. He wakes up before he has to.

Self-discipline isn’t performance — it’s how he knows his faith is real.

A man who can’t lead himself has nothing to offer the people who’d follow him.

  • He’s the same guy at home as he is with his brothers.
  • He’s not waiting for a woman to make him grow up.

A man under authority can be trusted with everything else.
