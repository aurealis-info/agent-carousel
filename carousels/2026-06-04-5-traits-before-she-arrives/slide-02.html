Chase God first

You can’t lead a woman closer to God if you’re not moving toward him yourself.

The guy who wants a wife more than he wants God will pull her in the wrong direction.

  • His daily time with God isn’t negotiable — even when he doesn’t feel it.
  • He’s in the Word before he’s in her DMs.

A man running after God doesn’t have time to be desperate.
