Planted in real men

Not a podcast. Not a group chat. Two men who have his number and know his actual life.

A man alone dulls slowly — and never sees it happening.

Iron sharpens iron. He shows up for them, too.

  • His brothers have permission to ask the hard question.
  • He’s not arriving at a relationship dragging unprocessed sin with no one holding him accountable.

You can’t be the man she needs if no man knows who you actually are.
