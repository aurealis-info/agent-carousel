Step 4

The ability to be still

Psalm 46:10: “Be still, and know that I am God.”

The man who can’t sit with quiet sprints toward distraction — screens, noise, or the next girl who makes the emptiness quiet for a while.

A man planted in God doesn’t need the relationship to fill what only God fills.

What you run to when you’re restless tells you everything.
