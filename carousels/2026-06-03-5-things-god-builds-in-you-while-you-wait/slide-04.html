Step 3

Faithfulness in small things

Luke 16:10: “Whoever is faithful in very little is also faithful in much.”

The man who leads a home is the man who first led himself.

  • How you treat the checkout clerk. Whether you follow through when no one’s watching.
  • Leadership isn’t a role you get. It’s a habit you build.

She’s not looking for a man with plans. She’s looking for a man with a track record.
