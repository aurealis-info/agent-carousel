Start Small. Start Now.

One thing. A chapter. Twenty minutes. Cold water before the phone. Not the hardest thing — just one thing that earns the day before the world asks for it.

You're not trying to become a monk. You're trying to be a man who shows up — for himself first, then for everyone else.

"Whatever you do, work at it with all your heart, as working for the Lord." — Colossians 3:23. Even the 6 AM counts.
