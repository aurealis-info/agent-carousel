Step 2

Run. And mean it.

"Flee youthful passions." — 2 Timothy 2:22

Paul didn't say manage them. He said move your body out of the room. Joseph ran from Potiphar's wife. He didn't talk himself out of it. The command is geographic.

  • What room are you still standing in?
  • Leaving is a decision, not a feeling.

The fastest move you make this week might be the best one.
