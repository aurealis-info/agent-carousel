The myth: stronger willpower

Most men treat temptation like a test of strength. Grip tighter, resist harder, count on themselves. That approach drains — and when it fails, they conclude they're broken.

They're not. They're using the wrong tool.

  • Willpower is a finite resource. It runs out.
  • Scripture doesn't call you to stand and endure. It calls you to flee.

You're not weak for feeling the pull. You're weak for standing in front of it.
