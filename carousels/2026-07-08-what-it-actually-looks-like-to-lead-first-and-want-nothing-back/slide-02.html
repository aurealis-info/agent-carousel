Step 1

Go first with the thing nobody wants

Christ washed feet before He gave a single command. Leadership starts in the unglamorous chore, not the title.

If you're waiting for the room to notice before you move, you're not leading yet.

John 13:14: "If I then, your Lord and Teacher, have washed your feet, you also ought to wash one another's feet."

  • Take the job nobody's fighting for.
  • Do it before you're asked twice.

The first move is always yours to make.
