Step 3

Let the win stay quiet

Jesus told the healed man to tell no one, more than once. The work spoke. He didn't need the credit to.

A godly man can do the hard thing and let it stay unannounced.

  • Do the good thing where no one's counting.
  • Let the result speak instead of you.

What you don't announce, God still sees.
