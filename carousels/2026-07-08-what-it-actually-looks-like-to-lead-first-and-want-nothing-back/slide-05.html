Step 4

Serve the man who can't repay you

Real leadership shows up for the guy with nothing to offer back. That's where the actual test is.

Anyone will go first for someone watching. Fewer will go first for someone who can't pay them back.

  • Find the one relationship with zero upside.
  • Stay in it anyway.

Lead where there's nothing in it for you. That's how you know it's real.
