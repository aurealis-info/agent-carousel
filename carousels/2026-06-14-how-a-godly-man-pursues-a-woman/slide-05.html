Step 4

Hold it
open-handed

There's a difference between a man pursuing a woman and a man clutching an outcome.

Pursue her. Lead her well. Then trust God with the answer. If you can't hold this open-handed, you can't hold anything well.

  • The desperate man repels what the surrendered man receives.
  • "Delight yourself in the Lord, and he will give you the desires of your heart." — Psalm 37:4

The man who needs her least loves her best.
