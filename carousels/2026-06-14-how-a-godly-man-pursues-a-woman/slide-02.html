Settle yourself
before you move

A man who needs her to fix his soul is already too needy to lead her.

Before you pursue her, check what you're bringing. Not a checklist — your actual state. Are you chasing God or running from something?

  • You can't give her what you don't have.
  • A man full of God brings something. An empty man just takes.
