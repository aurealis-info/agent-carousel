Step 3

Go first into the cost

Every man worth anything in a relationship learned to go first —

first to apologize, first to serve, first to take the hit.

Not because he's weak. Because the cost is where the character is forged.

"Greater love has no one than this: to lay down one's life for his friends." — John 15:13

First to go. Last to need anything back.
