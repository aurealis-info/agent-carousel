Step 5

Govern your cravings

Food, porn, the third drink, the late-night scroll.

Every craving you hand over trains your body to expect it. Every time you say no, you reclaim ground.

“I discipline my body and keep it under control.” — 1 Corinthians 9:27

  • Pick one craving. Say no to it today.
  • Not forever. Just tonight.

A man who governs his body can be trusted with everything else.
