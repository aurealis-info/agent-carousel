Step 2

Govern your eyes

Lust doesn’t start when she walks in.

It starts in the second glance, the scroll you let keep going, the tab you know you shouldn’t open.

“Everyone who looks at a woman with lustful intent has already committed adultery with her in his heart.” — Matthew 5:28

  • Know your triggers. Name them.
  • Bounce your eyes. Don’t negotiate.

Govern the glance before it governs you.
