Step 4

Govern your pride

When you’re overlooked. When someone else gets the credit. When you’re corrected in front of people.

How you respond in those moments tells you more about your sanctification than your Sundays do.

Pride dies slow. But it dies when you stop protecting it.

  • Let the correction land before you defend.
  • Ask: am I protecting my image or pursuing truth?

The man who can be corrected can be trusted.
