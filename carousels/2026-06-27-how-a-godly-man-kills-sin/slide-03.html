Bring it into the light

"Confess your sins to one another, that you may be healed." — James 5:16

This isn't advice. It's a command with a mechanism built in. Confession is the weapon, not a side note to the strategy.

What you say out loud to a brother starts dying in the light. What you keep private stays alive. The secrecy is part of how it holds on.
