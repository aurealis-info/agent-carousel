  1. 1. His mouth slows down.

    The man who talks to God a lot doesn't feel the need to fill every silence. He's already said the important thing.

  2. 2. His fear gets smaller.

    Not because the thing disappears — because he's put it in the right hands. Philippians 4:6–7.

  3. 3. He stops performing.

    Prayer strips the audience out. The man who's been honest with God has nothing left to perform for anyone else.

  4. 4. His anger cools faster.

    Hard to sustain a grudge against someone you've just prayed for. Try it. It's nearly impossible.
