Step 4

What to do with the ache

The longing is legitimate. Don't bury it — give it to God. Not as a transaction, but as trust.

Then go be useful. Go be planted. Go build the kind of man worth knowing. Stop auditing every room you walk into for a wife.

  • Let the ache push you toward God, not toward bad decisions.
  • The man chasing God becomes the man worth chasing.

She's not what completes you. He already did that.
