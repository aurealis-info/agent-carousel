Step 1

The desire is real — and God put it there

You're not wrong for wanting a companion. You're not less spiritual because you feel the ache.

But the guy who leads with his need — who walks into every room auditing the women in it — reeks of it. She reads it in the first five minutes.

  • Desperation is a scent, not a strategy.
  • A need you wear on your face is a need that runs people off.

The want isn't the problem. What you're doing with it is.
