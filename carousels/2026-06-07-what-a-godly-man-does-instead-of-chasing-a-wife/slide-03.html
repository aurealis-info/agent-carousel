This season is building you, not stalling you

You're not waiting for your life to start. This is your life.

The disciplines you build when no one's watching — the prayer, the work, the integrity when it costs you something — those are what make you the man worth marrying.

  • She's not what makes you ready. Being built is what makes you ready.
  • The man you are at 25 is the foundation she stands on at 35.

Use the unsupervised years like they're your formation. They are.
