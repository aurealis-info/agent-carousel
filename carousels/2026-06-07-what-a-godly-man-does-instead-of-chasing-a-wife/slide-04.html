Step 3

Stop treating her as the destination

A man who chases a wife as his highest aim often ends up with the wrong woman — and neither of them know it until the damage is done.

You're called to love her like Christ loved the church — and that requires a man who's already running toward God, not toward her.

  • If marriage is your chief end, you've already made an idol of it.
  • If Christ is, everything else — including her — lands where it belongs.

"Seek first the kingdom." He wrote that before you had this problem.
