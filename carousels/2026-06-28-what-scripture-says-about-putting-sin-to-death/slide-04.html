Step 3

You need a plan, not just resolve

Most men try to fight sin with willpower on the days it comes for them hardest. That's fighting a war with no strategy.

The man who's actually winning built a structure: blocked the path, told a brother, replaced the habit. He didn't leave the choice to his feelings at 11 PM.

  • What does your actual plan look like? Write it out.
  • If you can't name it, you don't have one yet.

Intentions don't kill sin. Structures do.
