Step 4

The Spirit does the actual killing

Romans 8:13 doesn't say "by your willpower." It says "by the Spirit." The killing is God's work done through a man who cooperates with it.

You do the hard work of structure, confession, and staying in the Word. The Spirit does what you can't do on a bad night alone. Both are required. Neither is enough without the other.

  • Your job: show up, build the structure, stay in the light.
  • His job: transform what your effort can't reach.

You cooperate with what God is killing. That's the deal.
