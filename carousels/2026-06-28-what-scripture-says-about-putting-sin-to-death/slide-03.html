Step 2

Killing it starts with exposing it

Light is the first weapon. What you've confessed to one man has already lost most of its grip on you.

You don't tell a brother so he can fix you. You tell him because darkness is where sin breathes. Take away the dark.

  • James 5:16 — "Confess your sins to one another, that you may be healed."
  • One man who knows the real war changes everything.

The confessed sin starts to die. The hidden one grows.
