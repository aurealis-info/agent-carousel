Step 1

Managing it keeps it alive

"Working on it" is how men keep sin comfortable and never face it directly.

A man who manages pornography still has it. A man who manages his anger still loses his temper. Management is coexistence, and the Bible doesn't offer coexistence as an option.

  • Romans 8:13 — "by the Spirit you put to death the misdeeds of the body."
  • Not reduce. Not manage. Put to death.

If you're still managing it, you haven't killed it yet.
