Step 3

Build for the long game

You're in your 20s. The 20s are for getting built, not for performing for every girl who makes eye contact.

Ask: what kind of man do you want to be at 35? Start becoming him now. The life you build is what a good woman is actually looking for. The marriage is a product of the man, not the other way around.

  • Work on your character harder than your presence.
  • A man with direction is magnetic. A man in limbo isn't.
