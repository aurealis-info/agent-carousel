Step 3

Build yourself first

Learn to handle money. Show up when you don't feel like it. Keep your word — to yourself, before anyone else.

The man nobody can count on isn't ready to be someone's partner. Build something worth inviting her into.

  • A woman doesn't complete a man. She joins one.
  • Build now, while you still have to prove it to yourself.

Don't invite someone into a life that isn't built yet.
