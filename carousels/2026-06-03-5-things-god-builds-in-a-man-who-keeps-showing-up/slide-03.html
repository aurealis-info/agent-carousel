Step 2

A body that does what you say

Your body has been running this show long enough.

The man who trains his flesh — the alarm, the gym, the phone, the meal — becomes a man his soul can actually move.

  • You don’t find self-control. You build it at 5 AM when you’d rather stay down.
  • One day at a time. Then ten. Then a year. Then you’re a different man.

The man who governs his body governs his life.
