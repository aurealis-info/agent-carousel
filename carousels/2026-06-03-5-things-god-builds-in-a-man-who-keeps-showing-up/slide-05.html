Step 4

Knowing what you’re made of

Showing up consistently teaches you things about yourself that analysis never will.

You find out what you actually believe when there’s no audience. What you’re actually afraid of. What you’re actually built for.

A man who avoids the work stays a stranger to himself. A man who faces it owns something no one can take.

Clarity doesn’t come from thinking. It comes from doing.
