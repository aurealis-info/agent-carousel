Step 1

Faithful before it matters

Before God trusts you with more, he watches what you do with what you already have.

Not the big moment. The assignment nobody would’ve checked. The task you finished clean when cutting corners was easier.

Most men are waiting for the big assignment. God’s already given them one.

“Whoever can be trusted with very little can also be trusted with much.” — Luke 16:10
