Two Men Who Have Your Number

Not a podcast. Not a Discord. Not a men's group where everyone says "I'm good" and means "I'm not, but let's not go there."

Two or three actual men who know your name, know your sin, show up when things break, and ask the hard question without flinching.

"As iron sharpens iron, so one person sharpens another." — Proverbs 27:17. You can't be sharpened by something that never touches you.
