Brotherhood Builds You

The man who lets real accountability in becomes a different kind of man. Not weaker — more capable.

Because now he has people who can see the blind spot he'll never find on his own. He has men who call him forward instead of letting him drift. He has somewhere to take the weight when it gets heavy.

That's not neediness. That's the design. Needing the right people — men who will tell you the truth and stay — is what strength looks like when it's honest.
