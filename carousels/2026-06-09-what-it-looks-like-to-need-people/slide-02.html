Isolation Is Not Strength

A man without accountability is a man without a mirror.

You can be disciplined, doctrinally sharp, and consistent at the gym — and completely blind to the thing that's slowly rotating you off course. Not because you're weak. Because you're alone.

God didn't design men to function in isolation. He designed them to sharpen each other. A man who won't let that happen has decided he knows himself better than anyone else can.

He's always wrong about that.
