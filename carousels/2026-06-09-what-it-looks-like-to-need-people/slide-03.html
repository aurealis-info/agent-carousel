What He's Actually Running From

When a man keeps everyone at arm's length, it's rarely self-sufficiency.

It's usually unconfessed sin he can't risk someone seeing. Or pride that can't take correction. Or wounds he's still pretending aren't there, because if he lets a man see them, he has to admit they're real.

Isolation is almost always protection. The question is: what are you protecting — yourself, or your sin?
