Step 2

Renew the mind, don't just resist the urge

Romans 12:2 doesn't say resist temptation. It says be transformed by the renewing of your mind.

White-knuckling the urge at 11 p.m. is the wrong fight. Feeding your mind something else earlier in the day is the real one.

  • Put something true in front of your eyes before the scroll gets the first word.

Win the morning and the midnight fight gets a lot shorter.
