Step 1

The first sign it's changing you wrong

The circle shrinks to people who agree with you. The man who would tell you the truth — you see him less. The friend who knew you before any of this — harder to reach.

That's not success. That's the slow drift that comes before a hard fall.

  • If everyone around you only affirms you, that's a warning.
  • Shrinking the circle is not protection. It's isolation with good lighting.

The man who can't hear hard truth anymore is already in trouble.
