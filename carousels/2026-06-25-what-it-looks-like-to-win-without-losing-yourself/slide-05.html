Step 4

The platform is a tool, not a destination

The man who thinks the win is the point will shrink to protect it. Every decision goes through the lens of what maintains it. That's not freedom. That's a new master.

The man who knows it came from God can hold it open-handed.

  • Steward what God gave you like a servant, not an owner.
  • What He gave, He can take. What He asked you to do with it — that's the only thing that stays.

"Before a downfall the heart is haughty, but humility comes before honor." — Proverbs 18:12
