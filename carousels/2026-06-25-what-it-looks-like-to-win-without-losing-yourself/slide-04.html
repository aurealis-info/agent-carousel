Step 3

Keep the man who knew you before

One man who met you when you weren't going anywhere. Who has no reason to manage you or protect the image. Who will say the hard thing on a Thursday night and still be there Friday morning.

That man is not a luxury. He's protection.

  • Call him. Let him see you in the ordinary moments.
  • Don't upgrade your circle out of the people who knew the unfinished version of you.

A man without accountability in a good season is setting up a hard fall.
