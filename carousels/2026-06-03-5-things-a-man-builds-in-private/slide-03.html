Thing 2

The ability to sit still

Most men can’t be alone for ten minutes without a screen.

The loneliness, the restlessness, the itch they can’t name — they fill it instead of facing it.

The man who learns to sit with hard things stops being run by them.

  • Next time you reach for the phone — don’t. Not yet.

Discomfort is the teacher you’ve been skipping.
