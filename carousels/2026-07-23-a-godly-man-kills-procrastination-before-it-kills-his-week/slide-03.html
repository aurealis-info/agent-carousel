Step 2

Feeling ready was never the requirement

Colossians 3:23 doesn't wait for motivation. Whatever you do, work heartily, as for the Lord and not for men. That includes the task you're circling right now.

There's no verse that excuses the inbox you still haven't opened.

  • Waiting to feel like it is how whole weeks disappear.
  • Obedience starts before the feeling does, not after.

Start before you feel ready. You were never going to feel ready.
