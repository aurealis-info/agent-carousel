Step 1

Name the thing you keep circling

Every week has one. The email sitting in drafts since Tuesday. The conversation you've rehearsed but haven't had. The workout you keep moving to tomorrow.

It's rarely the hardest thing on your list. It's just the one costing you the most attention while it sits there undone.

  • Write down the one task you've dodged the longest.
  • Notice how much room it's taking up in your head.

What you avoid doesn't go away. It just gets louder.
