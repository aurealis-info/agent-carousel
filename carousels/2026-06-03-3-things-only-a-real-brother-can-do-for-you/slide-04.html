Thing 3

Show up when you go silent

The measure of a real brother isn't what he does when you call.

It's what he does when you stop.

"A friend loves at all times, and a brother is born for a time of adversity." — Proverbs 17:17

You're not looking for someone to help when you ask. You're looking for someone who already knows to show up.

The men you need don't wait for an invitation.
