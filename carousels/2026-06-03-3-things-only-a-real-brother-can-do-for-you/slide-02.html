Thing 1

Name your sin out loud

You've journaled it. Prayed about it. Called it a "struggle" for two years.

But until you say it to a man who can look you in the eye — you haven't actually confessed it.

"Confess your sins to one another, that you may be healed." — James 5:16

  • One man. Eye to eye. The real version.
  • Not a small-group check-in. A brother.

Confession only heals when a real person hears it.
