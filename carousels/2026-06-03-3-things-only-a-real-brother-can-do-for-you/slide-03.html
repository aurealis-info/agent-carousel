Thing 2

Pull you back when you drift

You will drift. Not maybe — definitely.

It starts as busyness. Then it's pride. Then you haven't opened your Bible in six weeks and you're not sure how it happened.

The brother who calls you back doesn't wait for you to ask. He notices the quiet and he picks up the phone.

  • Ask him: "When did you last actually pray?"
  • Give him permission to do the same to you.

You can't see your own drift. God gave you men for that.
