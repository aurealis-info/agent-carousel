Step 3

Get your standard from the Word

A man shaped by Scripture knows what he's looking for: a woman who fears God, who has character under pressure, who makes him more — not comfortable.

That's not a checklist. It's a filter. And it only makes sense to a man who's already living the same standard himself.

"Charm is deceitful, and beauty is vain, but a woman who fears the Lord is to be praised." — Proverbs 31:30
