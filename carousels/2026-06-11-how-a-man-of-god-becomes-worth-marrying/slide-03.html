Step 2

She's not your sanctification

A lot of men think a wife will fix what Scripture is supposed to fix — the loneliness, the lack of purpose, the restlessness at 1 AM.

She won't. She'll just live inside those things with you. And that's a weight no woman should carry into a covenant.

  • Deal with your wounds before you drag them into a marriage.
  • A healed man doesn't need her to complete him. He's already whole.
