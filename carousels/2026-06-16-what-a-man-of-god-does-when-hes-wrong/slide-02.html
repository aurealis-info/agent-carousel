Step 1

Say it plainly

"I was wrong." Three words. Nothing after them.

You've heard the soft version: "I'm sorry if you felt..." That's not an apology. It's a defense wearing a mask. Own it clean or don't own it at all.

  • No qualifiers. No "but."
  • Say exactly what you did wrong, not a version of it.
