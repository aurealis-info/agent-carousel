Step 2

Don't wait to feel ready

You're never going to feel ready to humble yourself. Pride doesn't have a good time.

The wait is usually just you hoping time buries it. It doesn't bury it. It just gives your sin more room to root deeper.

  • Do it today, not when the tension cools.
  • Feeling ready is not the bar. Obedience is.
