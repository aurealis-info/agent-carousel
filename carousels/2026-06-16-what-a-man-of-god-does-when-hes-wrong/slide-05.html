Step 4

Let it be over

Once you've owned it, don't pick it back up. Some men apologize and then relitigate the whole thing to prove they were also right. That's not repair. That's a second fight wearing the costume of the first.

Say it. Mean it. Let the other person hold whatever they need to hold. You've done your part.

The man who can lose a point is the man worth following.
