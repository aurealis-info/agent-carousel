Step 3

Pay the relational cost first

Real leadership means you absorb the awkward silence, the rejection if it comes, the repair work nobody applauds.

You go first into the discomfort. Not because you'll be rewarded for it. Because that's what Christ modeled on every page of the gospel.

"Be kind to one another, tenderhearted, forgiving one another, as God in Christ forgave you." Ephesians 4:32.
