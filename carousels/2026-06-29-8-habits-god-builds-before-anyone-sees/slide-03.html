  1. 5. Doing the hard thing first.

    Every morning has a thing you want to avoid. A man of God builds the habit of going toward it. That character moves into every room he enters.

  2. 6. Sitting with Scripture long enough to be unsettled by it.

    Read until something convicts. Then stop. Let it land.

  3. 7. Moving your body even when motivation hasn't shown up.

    Showing up for the physical work on the days you don't feel like it is a spiritual discipline.

  4. 8. Texting one brother what's actually going on.

    Not a prayer request emoji. The real thing. Light is the only thing that breaks a grip — and light needs a messenger.
