Step 1

Remember the room test

Anyone can be decent to your face. That costs nothing.

The real read on a man is what he says the second you leave. If it changes, the face-to-face version was the act.

  • Character is what survives your absence.
  • If you wouldn't say it to him, don't say it about him.

You find out who a man is by who he is when you're not there to hear it.
