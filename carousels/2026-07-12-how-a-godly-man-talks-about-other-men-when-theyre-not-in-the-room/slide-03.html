Step 2

Don't trade in what he told you in confidence

A brother told you something because he trusted you with it, not the group chat.

Repeating it, even just to have something to say, sells him for small talk.

  • If it's not yours to tell, it doesn't leave your mouth.
  • Guarding his story is how he learns you'll guard your own.

Trust is built in the conversations no one else hears about.
