Step 4

Say the hard thing to him, not about him

If something's actually wrong, Scripture's route is his ear first, not everyone else's.

Matthew 18 says go to him, alone, before it becomes a topic. That's harder and it's the only version that's honest.

  • Talking around him is easier. Talking to him is love.
  • The same guy in the room and out of it: that's the whole test.

Say it to his face or don't say it at all.
