Step 3

Correct it instead of laughing along

The group is tearing someone down and it's easier to go quiet, or worse, laugh.

A godly man doesn't need to win the room by costing someone else something.

  • Silence in that moment is still a vote.
  • One flat "that's not fair" changes the room more than you'd think.

You don't have to make a speech. You just can't laugh along.
