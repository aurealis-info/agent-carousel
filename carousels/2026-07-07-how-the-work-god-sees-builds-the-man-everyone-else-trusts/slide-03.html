Step 2

Stop keeping score with the metrics

Likes, followers, the number in your account. None of it tells you whether the work was actually good.

Colossians says work at it with all your heart, as working for the Lord, not for the room.

  • Ask if the work was faithful, not if it was noticed.

Whatever you do, work at it as working for Him. (Colossians 3:23)
