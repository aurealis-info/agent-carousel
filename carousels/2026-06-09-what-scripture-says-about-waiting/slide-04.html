Step 3

What you do with the desire matters

The desire for a wife, a role, a calling — those aren't problems to suppress. God put them there.

But a man who makes the desire the center of his prayers is chasing the gift, not the Giver. Bring it. Hold it open-handed.

  • Ask for it honestly.
  • Then trust the timing to Someone with better sight lines than you.
