Step 1

Waiting is an active word

"Wait on the Lord" — in the Hebrew, qavah means to twist together like a cord. To bind yourself to Him while you move.

That's not a man sitting on a couch hoping something changes. That's a man staying tethered to God while he does the work in front of him.

  • You're not waiting instead of working.
  • You're working while you wait.
